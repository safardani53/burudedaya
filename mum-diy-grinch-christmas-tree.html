Mum creates Grinch Christmas tree for just 35 using B&amp;M &amp; Asda bargains

A MUM has revealed how she made her own incredible Grinch themed Christmas tree for just £35.

Taking to the Facebook group Extreme Couponing & Bargains UK she shared a snap of the fiendishly green creation.

The festive fir has the same whimsical curve to it that you would expect to see in the original Dr Seuss book and curling his green fingers around it is the seasonal scrooge himself.

The mum explained that she had constructed the Grinch figure using a character mask and hands that she picked up on Amazon for just £12.

Meanwhile the decorations were purchased at Poundland, Asda and B&M with the project costing her a total of £35 to make.

Explaining her method, she wrote: “I Stuffed the mask, hands and Santa coat and took branches from the back of the tree and added it to the top to make it bend over.

“Not everyone’s taste but we love it 😂”

Fellow group members were seriously impressed with her post receiving over 53,000 likes, leaving the mum gobsmacked.

She later added: “WOW I’m overwhelmed with everyone’s super nice comments. Can’t keep up with the comments to thank you all ☺”
