'Sensational' 25-FOLD bet win nets punter almost 13,000 after 'bold' acca

THE unpredictability of football means landing a double, treble or small acca is tough enough.

Well one punter SOMEHOW landed a monster 25-FOLD acca over the weekend - and pocketed a healthy return off the back of it!

The William Hill bettor stuck £15 on the colossus bet, which covered much of the weekend's football.

At odds of 864/1, they pocketed a staggering £12,980.29 return in the process.

There was a mix of match results and picks from the over/under 1.5 goals market.

And in total, it covered ELEVEN different leagues across Europe.

While the fury surrounding Brentford's equaliser at the Emirates rumbles on, this punter was among those not complaining (Brentford and Manchester City fans also) as they waited on over 1.5 goals in the game.

Among the others from that market included Bayern Munich vs Bochum, Monaco vs PSG, Lecce vs Roma and Real Madrid vs Al-Hilal.

On to the match results and there were one or two that would've left the mystery punter sweating slightly.

Manchester United left it late through Marcus Rashford and Alejandro Garnacho to wrap up a 2-0 win over Leeds.

United at 10/11 was actually the longest odds on the accumulator which featured many long odds-on shots.

Rangers (1/14) needed a late goal to see off Championship side Partick Thistle in the Scottish Cup.

Manchester City's 3-0 half-time lead would've settled any nerves they had on that selection.

There wasn't too much drama heading into the later games on Sunday either, with Napoli at 2/11 winning 3-0.

But that wasn't long after the real heart-in-mouth tie in Portugal, when they needed over 1.5 goals in the game between Sporting and Porto.

Porto took the lead with half an hour to go and that looked like being it, until Pepe's 94th minute goal made it 2-0 to keep the miraculous punt alive - though Sporting did score five minutes later.

A bit of luck? Of course. But it seems certainly deserved given the rest of the acca before it.
