Buying Beverly Hills 2022 Season 1 Is Set To Be Released On Netflix

Buying Beverly Hills is a brand-new Netflix reality TV program that will launch on November 4, 2022, at 3.01 a.m. ET. The series is an “occu-soap” that follows the life of real-estate billionaire Mauricio Umansky. He is also taking his girls with him. Mauricio is hardly a fresh face on television. Kyle Richards, an RHOBH (Real Housewives of Beverly Hills) celebrity, is his wife.

According to the official synopsis for Buying Beverly Hills (Season 1):

“Mauricio Umansky’s family-run organization The Agency represents some of Beverly Hills’ most opulent houses.” But drama lurks at every turn.”

Buying Beverly Hills, 2022 touted “envy-inducing interiors, tears, and maybe a little romance” in a Netflix press release. Buying Beverly Hills will consist of eight 40-minute segments. It will involve “interpersonal turmoil and real estate wheeling and dealing,” according to Netflix. With just a few weeks before the next reality TV series premieres on the streaming service, here’s everything you need to know about it.

Season 1 of Buying Beverly Hills will include magnificent residences.

Mauricio is the CEO of The Agency, a Beverly Hills-based real estate corporation with offices across the globe. Farrah, Mauricio’s daughter, is one of the prominent advocates. She also works as a senior realtor for her father’s company. Alexia, Mauricio and Kyle’s daughter, is The Agency’s newest recruit and is making her way in the real estate profession. The plot centres on the three of them and their colleagues from The Agency. Buying Beverly Hills, like the other well-known reality TV real estate shows on Netflix, will include gorgeous residences. Viewers may also anticipate drama, romance, and opulent events organized by The Agency.

Before the episode debuted on Netflix, the streaming service teased viewers with a sneak glimpse of what to anticipate from the next season. In the clip, Mauricio tells his daughter Alexia,

“The more successful you are, the more garbage people will say about you.”

The teaser also provides fans with a taste of the drama to come. We can see in it that Alexia is concerned about not meeting expectations.

She revealed,

“I’m going to be terrified of doing a poor job.”

Melissa also inquired about Alexia’s interest in real estate. However, Melissa is also in difficulty because she believes someone is interfering with her employment. Above all, fans will get to watch Farrah and her boyfriend Alex having a meaningful debate about her future.

Who will feature in the first season of Buying Beverly Hills in 2022?

The primary stars of the upcoming reality TV show are Mauricio, Farrah, and Alexia. American Idol season 15 finalist Sonika Vaid, Ben Belack, Jon Grauman, Allie Lutz Rosenberger, Santiago Arana, Joey Ben-Zvi, and Melissa Platt join them.
