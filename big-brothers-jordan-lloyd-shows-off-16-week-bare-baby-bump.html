Big Brother's Jordan Lloyd Shows Off 16-Week Bare Baby Bump

Documenting her journey to baby No. 2! Jordan Lloyd shared her first baby belly shot just three days after sharing the news that she’s expecting her second child with husband Jeff Schroeder.

The 31-year-old Big Brother alum took to Instagram on Monday, March 26, to commemorate her 16-week bump and update fans on her very different second pregnancy experience.

“This time around I feel like my stomach popped sooner than it did with Lawson,” she wrote alongside a mirror picture that showed off her stomach. “My doctor told me it would & said it’s normal. Being nauseous is gone, but I’m still in that tired phase. Patiently waiting, & hoping it goes away soon since I’m in my 2nd trimester.”

Lloyd continued to detail her side-effects — which she said she never had with her 17-month-old son Lawson, whom she and Schroeder, 39, welcomed in October 2016.

“This time I’m battling the never ending pimples/boils on my chin. I get rid of one & then another pimple pops up in the morning😩. The headaches get to me because they never go away!!” she added, noting that “it doesn’t help when you have a screaming toddler around.”

She continued: “Besides that, everything else is going good so far! I go to the doctor Wednesday & find out what we are having!! So excited! This whole time I was thinking boy, & now I’m thinking girl. We will see…!!!”
