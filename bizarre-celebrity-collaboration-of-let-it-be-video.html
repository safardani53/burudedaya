Bizarre Celebrity Collaboration of &quot;Let It Be&quot; VIDEO

We have no other word than bizarre to explain the video below. To kick off its fourth season, Norwegian entertainment show Gylne Tider (Golden Times) pulled together the oddest line up of 80’s and 90’s “stars” (we use that word loosely) to sing or lip-sync to the Beatles song Let It Be.

There are too many names to list but you will see bad girl skater Tonya Harding, Alfonso Ribeiro, Glenn Close, Huey Lewis, Norm from Cheers, George from Seinfeld and 50 other entertainers singing in front of a beach backdrop. Even newly deceased Leslie Nielsen appeared.

Watch the video below and let us know your thoughts. It would be funny if it was a spoof on We Are the World but unfortunately it’s not.
