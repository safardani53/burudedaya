Jessica Simpson, Ashlee Simpson Double Date With Husbands

— Jessica Simpson and Ashlee Simpson made it a family affair with husbands Eric Johnson and Evan Ross, who all came out to support Joe Simpson for his first art show, Dreams Work, at Bruce Lurie Gallery in L.A.

— Pharrell Williams chilled with Big Sean and A$AP Rocky at his Adidas Originals x Pharrell Williams Pink Beach Collection Spring/Summer 2016 launch event in L.A.

— Ellen Pompeo and husband Chris Ivery munched on tomato tartare at the opening of Beauty & Essex at the Cosmopolitan Las Vegas.

— Empire star Kaitlin Doubleday picked out an all-white ensemble while shopping before her honeymoon with new husband Devin Lucien at Glamboutique on Melrose in L.A.
