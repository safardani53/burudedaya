Kate Moss, 49, and lookalike daughter Lila, 21, pose in matching jumpers and hats for Vogue magazine

WOOL you look at that! Kate Moss is joined by her lookalike daughter Lila as they pose together for Vogue magazine.

The supermodel, 49, and Lila, 21, wore knitted jumpers and matching hats for the shoot, which is part of the magazine’s December issue celebrating great Britons.

Kate used the interview to reveal her daughter has started to mother her, saying: “Now that she’s 21, Lila’s the one setting rules for me: wear SPF50; quit smoking… Although she did give me this cigarette case as a gift recently that I just love.”

Actresses Jodie Comer, 30, and Olivia Colman, as well as rapper Stormzy, 30, are among others to have landed covers of the fashion bible.

Last week, Kate was accused of apparently throwing a last-minute sickie to miss a star-studded bash.

This party wasn’t just any old knees up, either, it was thrown in Kate’s honour to celebrate her partnership with American designer Anine Bing who had splashed some serious cash at the do and had flown over from Los Angeles especially.

An insider said: “Anine and her team had pulled out all the stops for a mega-party to celebrate their partnership.

“Kate is the face of the brand’s autumn campaign and they had organised the massive party because it is such a major deal.

“They even had a London taxi with a huge picture of the advert on the side of it and had forked out large sums of money on booze and posh canapes.

“But not long after the party started they were told Kate couldn’t make it because she wasn’t well.
