Richard Samet "Kinky" Friedman is a singer, songwriter, novelist, comedian, politician and former columnist of Texas Monthly, who follows the stereotypes of famous American satirists Will Rogers and Mark Twain. Friedman was one of two independent candidates in the 2006 election for governor of Texas. Receiving 12.6% of the vote, Friedman placed fourth in the six-man race.
