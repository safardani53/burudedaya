Geordie Hospital viewers all say the same thing after heart-wrenching series finale

GEORDIE Hospital viewers were all left reaching for the tissues after a heart-wrenching finale on Tuesday night.

The fly-on-the-wall Channel 4 documentary showcases the dedication and hard work of some of our NHS heroes and follows the moving stories of the patients they care for.

Understandably, the show has become a massive hit amongst viewers, with many loving the format that showcases the NHS staff hard at work.

As Tuesday night's finale came to a close, viewers took to Twitter to recognise the tireless efforts that those in the hospital put in.

Alongside an emoji of a crying face, one person penned: "I knew the last episode of Geordie Hospital was going to pull at the heartstrings. I've blubbed three times already."

Echoing their comments, someone else said: "This is an amazing programme and is a shining example of our brilliant NHS."

While a third added: "So many emotions on the latest episode of Geordie Hospital. Not all heroes wear capes."

During its latest episode, Doris McGuire appeared in the finale as she underwent her second cataract operation - on her 86th birthday.

The pensioner and mother-of-two revealed that the staff at the hospital had made the "world of difference".

Everyone from porters to surgeons, dental surgeons, life-saving medics and even a welfare dog feature in Geordie Hospital.

All episodes of Geordie Hospital are now available to watch on All 4.
