Who is Olivia Plath? | The US Sun

TLC'S Welcome to Plathville follows Barry and Kim Plath and their nine kids.

Their son Ethan Plath married Olivia in October 2018, however, the two called it quits in October 2023, and here's everything we know.

Who is Olivia Plath?

Olivia Plath is a wedding photographer born on April 25, 1998, in Rocky Mount, Virginia.

Like the Plath family, Olivia grew up in a very conservative household. Olivia is one of 10 children living in a legalistic Christian home.

She and Ethan Plath met in 2014 during a family camping trip.

According to their wedding website, Olivia and Ethan maintained a long-distance relationship for the first three years.

On November 3, 2017, Ethan popped the question at an intimate dinner where they had their first date.

The following year, on October 13, 2018, the two officially tied the knot.

When did Olivia Plath and Ethan separate?

After five years of marriage, on October 27, 2023, Olivia revealed via Instagram that the two have decided to part ways.

In an Instagram post, Olivia wrote: "Ethan and I decided to go our separate ways.

"I have ambivalent memories of us being 18 years old, innocently dreaming of forever. But, the partner you choose is a reflection of the world you want to live in, and at 18 I didn't know who I was or what kind of life I wanted to create for myself.

"I've already begun my journey of questioning, deconstructing and rebuilding and know that is a process I will continue to prioritize throughout my life."

She ended her post addressing Ethan saying: "Thank you for the good times Ethan. I wish the best for you."

Shortly after, Ethan took to social media to share his own statement on their breakup.

"Olivia and I have parted ways. It just didn't work between us, though Lord knows we both gave it all we had," he began the statement.

"It seemed we were constantly pulling each other in opposite directions in life with different goals.

"I wish Olivia the best in the future ahead. I care for her deeply and always will."

What is Olivia Plath's net worth?

According to multiple sources, Olivia Plath has a net worth ranging between $100,000 to $500,000.

Distractify reported that Ethan has a net worth of $500,000.

While the two stir the pot with their drama on the show, Kim and ex-husband Barry Plath have the highest net worths of $3 million combined.
