You need to run, not walk, to Savers - Yankee Candles are scanning at more than HALF the price than

IF YOUR home is in need of an upgraded scent, then Savers might just have a rather affordable solution.

The discount hotspot is currently selling large Yankee Candles for just £10.99.

This is bargain compared to at Tesco, where similar candles from the brand will set you back £25.

The tip was shared by a shopper at Savers' Crewe branch in the Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group on Facebook.

A woman called Kim posted a photo that revealed the incredible selection of scents on offer.

On the well-stocked shelf could be seen options including 'amber musk' and 'banana flower' priced at just over a tenner.
