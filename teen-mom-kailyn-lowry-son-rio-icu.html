Teen Mom Kailyn Lowry shares never-before-seen photos of son Rio, 1, in NICU one year after she gave

TEEN Mom star Kailyn Lowry has posted photos of her one-year-old son's birth one year after he was born.

The 31-year-old secretly gave birth to her fifth child Rio last December.

Kailyn had him with her boyfriend Elijah Scott, 25.

On Friday, she made a new post on Instagram celebrating the birth of her son and sharing never-before-seen pictures of the experience.

The first picture showed Elijah sitting next to and looking down at his newborn son who was hooked up to oxygen and lying in a hospital bed.

The next picture showed Kailyn holding her baby while he was intubated.

The Teen Mom was wearing a mask and seemed to be holding her son very gently.

She also shared another photo of the medical equipment in the NICU without Rio.

The final picture showed the baby surrounded by a team of doctors and nurses.

They appeared to be performing tests and giving Rio medical care shortly after he was born.

THE WHOLE STORY

The MTV star shared her experience during her pregnancy with her fifth child during her podcast, Barely Famous. 

The couple spent the episode going into detail about finding out she was pregnant, buying several pregnancy tests, their first ultrasound, and the birthing process.

Elijah talked about the first doctor visit: "We went together and we were sitting in the room and you were supposed to be either six or seven weeks because of the little app she was using to track the pregnancy.

"The ultrasound tech came in, did the ultrasound, and didn't really say nothing she was just quiet and then you kept asking her and was like 'Is there a heartbeat?' and she said 'No, I can't find one' and then that's when you cried all weekend."

In the following weeks, they went back for a second ultrasound appointment when they confirmed the doctors found a heartbeat and she was indeed pregnant.

Kailyn labeled the birth as "traumatizing" and said they barely got to see him when he came out - clarifying she only got to hold him for "maybe three seconds."

The Teen Mom 2 star said there were also roughly six nurses who had "hands" on Rio as he was immediately taken to the NICU.
