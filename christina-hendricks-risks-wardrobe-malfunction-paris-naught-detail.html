Christina Hendricks, 48, risks wardrobe malfunction in dress- but fans distracted by 'naughty' detai

CHRISTINA Hendricks has nearly risked a wardrobe malfunction in a new post while fans were busy pointing out a background detail.

The Mad Men star took to her Instagram over the weekend to share a glimpse of her meal while in Paris, France.

Christina, 48, has been spending time in the city for Paris Fashion Week while flaunting her weight loss in glamorous outfits.

For her newest upload, she posed as she sat at a restaurant table in front of two plates consisting of a niçoise salad and french fries, as well as an Orangina to wash it down.

The Good Girls actress subtly smiled at the camera as she gestured at the plates, showing off her softly curled red hair and blue eyes.

For her outfit, Christina wore a black-and-white polka dot dress that allowed her to show off ample cleavage with its plunging neckline.

DOUBLE TAKE

PARIS FASHION WEEK

While attending Paris Fashion Week over the past few days, the actress was seen flaunting her figure and dramatic weight loss in a show-stopping ensemble.

The 48-year-old showed off her slimmer frame in a gorgeous layered gown at the Vivienne Westwood show on Saturday, where she sat front row next to Pamela Anderson.

She shared a few snaps from the event on her Instagram, letting her long red tresses fall from a bun as she smoldered in one photo.

The actress also wore a second look from the fashion house while attending their show - a floral dress with a patterned vest and quilt-like coat overtop.
