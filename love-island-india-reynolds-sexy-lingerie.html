Love Island star India Reynolds, 32, wows in sexy lingerie from her latest collection after freezing

LOVE Island frill-seeker India Reynolds models lingerie from her latest collection — and shows she is still a thriller four years after leaving the villa.

The 32-year-old posed for UK fashion label Pour Moi, saying her range “embodies beauty, confidence and empowerment”.

India was third in the 2019 series and dated basketball-playing winner Ovie Soko, but the pair broke up after a few months.

Last month she hinted she was single again while revealing she was freezing her eggs.

She added cheekily: “You can’t have sex but chance would be a fine thing around here.”

Writing on Instagram she said: "Thank you also to everyone that’s messaged support it’s really really helped with morale and made me feel so much less alone.”
