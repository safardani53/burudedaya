Inside Too Hot To Handle star Davids flat with huge brown sofa, very messy wardrobe and hundreds

INSIDE Netflix’s Too Hot To Handle star David Birtwistle’s London lad pad, complete with a huge leather L-shaped sofa, a flat screen TV in every room and hundreds of pairs of trainers.

The 30-year-old is a former semi-professional rugby player and the founder of coaching company Endeavour Life.

Thanks to Netflix’s new dating show Too Hot To Handle, the British public has a fresh series to binge watch during lockdown while they get to know the contestants.

This includes David Birtwistle, a former semi-professional rugby player for Coventry’s Wasps Rugby Club.

Based in London, where he runs his own coaching company, Endeavour Life, the hunky fitness addict has been showing off his lockdown workouts on Instagram and giving his followers a glimpse into his Clapham home.

David, a Nike Training ambassador, has plenty of space in his flat to work out and likes nothing more than to get down and dirty on the floor of his living room.

Featuring a huge brown leather sofa, which is L-shaped to make the most of the area, it also contains a large flat screen TV on the wall to watch Too Hot To Handle on repeat.

David doesn’t seem to have much in the way of ‘stuff’ cluttering up his lad pad with just a few comfy cushions on the sofa and a few photos on the wall.

However, when it comes to the hunk’s bedroom it seems this is where he hides his vast trainer collection.

In one photo there seems to be racks of trainers with many, many more slotted in under his cosy-looking bed. And of course, no lad pad would be complete without a very messy wardrobe to hold all his tight t-shirts.

Behind David's bed, just in case he forgets where he lives, is a mural of London's skyline featuring some of the capital's most famous landmarks.

Although he hasn't given fans a peek inside his bathroom yet, he has treated them to a little look of one of his favourite rooms in his flat – the kitchen.

Looking ripped in just a pair of grey tracksuit bottoms and a cap, David's minimal kitchen has wooden worktops and clean white walls and tiles.

And even though the 30-year-old is very much a healthy eater, even he needs a microwave to heat up his beans.
