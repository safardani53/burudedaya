Kris Jenner shows off new $3K GOLD Louis Vuitton x Nike sneakers after fans slam family for flauntin

KRIS Jenner has shown off her new $3,000 gold Louis Vuitton x Nike sneakers.

The opulent display comes after fans have slammed her famous family for flaunting their wealth.

Taking to her Instagram Stories, 66-year-old Kris showed off her new pair of shoes that come with a hefty price tag.

The gold sneakers were designed in collaboration with Louis Vuitton and Nike, adding a new layer of luxury to the streetwear classic Air Force 1s.

The lace-ups are gilded with Louis Vuitton’s signature monogram, and Nike brand detailing that adds an artistic touch.

They were designed by the late Off White CEO Virgil Abloh, who passed away in 2021 aged 41.

Virgil was close with the Kardashian family, and last month, Kylie Jenner, 24, wore an Off White wedding dress to the Met Gala to pay tribute to the modern fashion icon.

The new sneakers, along with a range of similar Air Force 1 editions are available at Louis Vuitton stores for a whopping $3k.

But they will form just a small part of Kris’s extensive shoe collection.

The momager has a whole room in her giant walk-in closet dedicated to just footwear.

There are walls and rows of boots, sneakers, and high-heels which are organized by color and style to the Hulu star’s liking.

But even among her $2,000 Gucci boots, $800 Chanel heels and limited edition Yeezy’s, the new gold sneakers will be one of the most expensive pairs of kicks in her collection.

FAMILY FORTUNE

Never afraid to splash the cash, Kris and her family have faced backlash from some Kardashian fans for the way they flaunt their wealth. 

Earlier this month, Khloe Kardashian, 37, angered fans as she posed in her Rolls Royce surrounded by luxury shopping bags.

For the pic, Khloe sat inside the $350,000 car, surrounded by bags from designers including Louis Vuitton, Bottega, and Chanel.

On Reddit, one user described it as “poor taste” while another slammed it as “tacky”.

Similarly, Kylie was criticized this month for showing off her $210,000 Birkin bag before boarding her $72m private jet.

KUWTK fanatics had opinions about Kylie showing off her elaborate fortunes  - namely her pricey Hermès accessory.
