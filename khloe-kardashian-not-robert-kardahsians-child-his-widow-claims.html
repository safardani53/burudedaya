Khloe Kardashian NOT Robert Kardahsian's Child, His Widow Claims

It is hard to do stories like these because of the damage they can do. It is being reported in Star magazine that Khloe Kardashian is not the late Robert Kardashian‘s biological child.

Jan Ashley, who married Robert after he and Kris Jenner divorced, revealed the information saying, “Khloe is not his kid — he told me that after we got married. He just kind of looked at me and said like it was a matter of fact. He said, ‘Well, you know that Khloe’s not really a Kardashian, don’t you?’ And I said…’OK,’ and that was it.”

After divorcing Jan Ashley, Robert had a six year relationship with Ellen Pierson and married her six weeks prior to his death in 2003.

Ellen had this to say about the paternity question, “Khloe brought it up all the time. She looked nothing like the rest. She was tall, had a different shape, light hair, curly hair. Didn’t look anything like the other three children.”

Kris did admit that she had cheated on Robert in her new book: Kris Jenner…and All Things Kardashian. Other than the admission of cheating she did not say anything about Khloe.

Robert’s widow Ellen said, “Robert did question whether Khloe was his. Any normal man would if they knew their wife had cheated on him. But he never would have considered a DNA test. He loved her very much.”
