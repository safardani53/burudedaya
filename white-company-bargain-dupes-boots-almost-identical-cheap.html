IF you are a fan of luxury beauty products but are trying to cut down on your spending, you’ve come to the right place.

With the cost-of-living crisis putting a huge strain on many people and their purses, it seems as though more and more of us are trying to limit their spending where possible.

But don’t worry, doing so doesn’t mean that you can’t have the nice things in life, particularly when it comes to beauty products.

Thanks to high-street retailer Boots, you can now pick up some brilliant The White Company dupe products that are strikingly similar to the popular chain and a huge fraction of the price.

Freya Farrington, an interior and beauty fan, has 10.1k followers and 120.7k likes on TikTok and regularly shares her bargain finds to the video sharing platform.

In one of her recent videos, Freya spotted some dupes of the cult classic The White Company products, but fortunately, they don’t come with the hefty price tags.
