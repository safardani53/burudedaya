Laura Fraser Biography

In 2003, Laura got married to Karl Geary. Karl Geary is an actor from United States and he is also a club owner. He is famous for his role in Nadja, Hamlet and many more. As a loving spouse, they have been parents once. Besides this married life there is no any information about her extra affairs and boyfriend and there is no any rumor about her divorce.

Laura joined Hillhead High School to receive her formal education. To accomplish her acting dreams, she joined Scottish Youth Theatre and began learning acting. She also joined Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama. She made her acting debut in 1995 by starring in the short movie Good Day for the Bad Guys where she was found playing the role of Red Riding Hood. Next year she appeared in the movie Small Faces. She got her rise in fame after she starred the role of Door in the TV series Neverwhere. She is also famous for playing the role of Kate in the movie   A Knight's Tale. She has been earning lucrative amount of salary annually and her net worth is said to be around $3 Million.
