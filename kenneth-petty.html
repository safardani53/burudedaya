Kenneth Petty Convictions

In 2006, he shot and killed Lamant Robinson with a loaded handgun.

Authorities wanted to charge him with the second-degree murder of shooting Lamant, but he pleaded guilty and was charged with first-degree murder. He was imprisoned for 7 years and was released on parole in May 2016.

Kenneth is a registered sex offender. He was convicted of the first-degree attempted rape of a 16-year-old girl in 1995 and served time in a New York state prison for the crime.

He was required to register as a sex offender in the state. His legal problems came to light when he and Nicki went public with their relationship in 2018.

He got into trouble again in November 2019 during a traffic stop in Los Angeles.

In Sept. 2021, Kenneth pled guilty to the one count of failure to register as a sex offender charge, seemingly striking a plea deal.

He was sentenced on July 6, 2022.

Although it was initially reported that he could face up to ten years in prison, his sentencing was three years probation and one year of home confinement, a representative from the U.S. Attorney’s Office confirmed to HollywoodLife.

He also had to pay a $55,000 fine.
