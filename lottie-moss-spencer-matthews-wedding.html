LOTTIE MOSS has admitted “partying” with married TV star Spencer Matthews – but insists they did not have sex.

The OnlyFans model spoke out after videos claiming she hooked up with the former Made In Chelsea favourite, 35, clocked up more than 111million views on TikTok.

In an exclusive interview in today’s Fabulous magazine, Lottie, 25, denied being a homewrecker, saying: “Spencer’s a great guy.

"We partied together but there was nothing more to it.”

Rumours also spread on WhatsApp that the pair had got together behind the back of Spencer’s model wife Vogue Williams, 37, in May.

The alleged union was said to have taken place at pal Jamie Laing and Sophie Habboo’s wedding in Marbella, where Spencer was master of ceremonies.

The reality TV star, who previously admitted he quit booze for the sake of his marriage, attended the bash alone as Vogue messed up her diary and had a work commitment in Dublin.

Responding to the gossip, Lottie — who is the half-sister of supermodel Kate Moss, 49 — told Fabulous: “Oh my God. It’s not true.

“It didn’t happen. Why would I bother addressing something that has no truth to it?

"Everyone at that wedding knows it didn’t happen.

“That evening I was with Made In Chelsea’s Liv Bentley.

"Me and her were talking about it yesterday and she was like, ‘I was there, nothing happened’.

“But the rumour has got so crazy.

"At one point, they were saying he’d slept with Liv too. It was like Chinese whispers.

"I think it was because it was such a big wedding and it was full of all these reality stars, so people wanted some gossip.”

Lottie said she wanted to set the record straight in a bid to stop people quizzing her over the allegations and avoid further speculation on forums linked to websites such as Tattle Life and Reddit.

She added: “People will read it and say, ‘It must be true’.

"I’m just glad Liv and other people were with me the whole time so they can confirm it isn’t.”

As well as Spencer and Lottie, the wedding was attended by a host of celebrities, including pro dancer Oti Mabuse plus Made In Chelsea’s Millie Mackintosh, Hugo Taylor and Ollie Locke.

New Strictly signing Zara McDermott and boyfriend Sam Thompson were also there.

Lottie rose to fame after acting as Kate’s bridesmaid aged 13 — as her big sis married her now ex-husband Jamie Hince in 2011.

Photos from the big day appeared in Vogue magazine and top model agency Storm later signed the younger sibling.

Lottie has worked with US supermodel Gigi Hadid and shot campaigns for Dolce & Gabbana and Calvin Klein.

But she struggled to cope with her older sister’s fame, as well as the pressures of the modelling industry, and fell into drug abuse.

Following a stint in rehab in Arizona last year, Lottie, who earns her living on OnlyFans, is now sober.

She and Spencer have been pals for years.

Lottie previously dated his Made In Chelsea mate Alex Mytton in 2017.

But they split after nine months amid claims he repeatedly cheated on her.

Lottie previously revealed: “I have been cheated on multiple times.

"My ex used to do a lot of personal appearances and he cheated on me with a girl at uni and started crying.

“I got back with him and it was stupid.

"He came to meet me with a sweatshirt with ‘I’m sorry’ drawn on and he was playing Sorry by Justin Bieber.

"I was only 18. He then went on to do it again.

“The worst thing was I was having threesomes with him.”

Spencer was an original member of Channel 4’s Made In Chelsea cast in 2011.

He stayed for ten series, earning a reputation as a womaniser and claiming he had bedded more than 1,000 women.

But he was tamed by Irish star Vogue in 2017, when they both competed on reality show The Jump.

Meanwhile, Lottie is appearing in the new series of E4’s Celebs Go Dating and is rumoured to be dating Love Island star Adam Collard, 27.

The pair were spotted leaving trendy nightspot Chiltern Firehouse in London hand in hand last weekend.

Speaking to The Sun on Sunday on Friday, Liv said of Lottie’s rumoured romp with Spencer: “It is all a load of rubbish.

"Lottie was with me the whole night and nothing happened.

“Could you imagine being Vogue and hearing all of these ridiculous things?”

Also in her Fabulous interview, Lottie revealed she is not best buddies with Kate.
