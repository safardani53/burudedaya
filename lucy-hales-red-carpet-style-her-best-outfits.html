Lucy Hales Red Carpet Style: Her Best Outfits

We love Lucy! From glittery gowns to mini skirts, Lucy Hale never fails to command attention on the red carpet.

Hale got her big break on the small screen during season three of The O.C. in 2006, portraying boarding school student Hadley Hawthorne. In 2010, the mystery show Pretty Little Liars premiered, and the Tennessee native was cast as Aria Montgomery — an artsy teenager determined to discover what happened to her missing friend. The show aired for a strong seven seasons until concluding in 2017. Since then, the Hating Game star has appeared in several TV shows and movies including Truth or Dare, A Cinderella Story: Once Upon a Song, Fantasy Island and more.

Besides her stellar acting career, the Katy Keene star never fails to dazzle Us with her personal and red carpet style. Although she delivers drama in glamorous outfits for Hollywood events, Hale described herself as a “jeans, tee shirt, sneakers kind of girl … and a leather jacket,” in a February 2020 interview with The Jam TV Show.

The A Nice Girl Like You actress sported her go-to look in January 2020, when she was spotted out and about in Los Angeles. The “Make You Believe” singer rocked a cropped white T-Shirt, black leggings with a striped waistband and gray sneakers. She accessorized with a timeless leather jacket, hoop earrings, sunglasses and completed the look with her brunette locks twisted into a bun.

While Hale’s laid-back style is to-die-for, her red carpet looks are just as mesmerizing. The Dude star slayed in a Dolce & Gabbana gown at the Elton John AIDS Foundation Academy Awards Viewing Party in March 2022. Her glitzy getup featured a one-shoulder neckline, a bedazzled corset bodice, figure hugging skirt, thigh-high slit and fringe cascading down her shoulder.

For glam, the Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants 2 actress donned a dewy face, warm eyeshadow shades, subtle contour, feathered eyebrows, peachy cheeks and rosy lips. Her hair was parted down the middle and pulled into a chic updo and to top it odd, the Scream 4 star accessorized with minimalistic rings and hoop earrings.

More recently, Hale looked edgy and elegant at the Fashion Trust U.S. Awards in March 2023. The Life Sentence star wore a floral Versace piece finished with a scoop neckline, thigh-high slit, ruffled skirt and the Versace logo. She completed the look with fuchsia eyeshadow, a choker necklace and black platform heels.
