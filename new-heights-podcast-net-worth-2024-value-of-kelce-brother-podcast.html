How Did New Heights Begin?

It was in 2020 that Travis Kelce’s longtime manager, Aaron Eanes, came up with the idea of New Heights. Hours after the playoff game, the Kelce brothers sat at a steakhouse and started discussing the game.

Seeing the two brothers’ connection, Eanes thought he could project it to millions of viewers and listeners. After the meal, the three of them became serious about the topic, and two years later, they came up with New Heights.  

The trio held conversations with several companies, and finally, they landed on Wave Sports + Entertainment. Travis and Jason record remotely on Tuesday, usually a day off for NFL players, and a team of WSE employees edits, post-produces, and releases the show on Wednesday. 

Previously, in an interview, Travis said, “It’s been good to just have that set amount of time to be able to just sit down and chat.

It’s like we’re just kicking it at dinner back when we were in high school and college together.”
