Naked in That Awkward Moment Trailer!

That Awkward Moment? Try that AMAZING moment!

In the new red-band trailer for That Awkward Moment – a film that features Zac Efron, Michael B. Jordan and Miles Teller as friends who vow to stay single – the former High School Musical star bares it all in one especially hot scene.

It depicts Efron sprawled across a toilet after suffering side effects as the result of taking Viagra – and while we don’t see ay of his naughtiest bits, it’s nude footage that would make Miley Cyrus proud.

 

Efron, of course, recently left a rehab facility after overdosing on hillbilly heroin, the street name for oxycodone.

So while it’s awfully nice to see the young star in his birthday suit, we’re really just relieved that we see him at all.

Efron talked about the movie during a fan Q&A in Culver City yesterday and said of the comedy:

"The movie was about girls and friends and relationships and [shows] kind of how our lives are, so I think we knew a lot about it and I think we had almost too much fun. It was great."
