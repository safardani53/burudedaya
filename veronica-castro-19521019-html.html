Verónica Castro, full name Verónica Judith Sáinz Castro Alba, is a Mexican actress, singer, producer, former model and presenter. She is the mother of singer Cristian Castro and filmmaker Michel Castro. She began her career as a television actress, where she met comedian Manuel Valdés, the father of her son Cristian Castro, and in fotonovelas and telenovelas while obtaining a degree in international relations.
