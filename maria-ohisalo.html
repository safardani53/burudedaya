Maria’s childhood was marked by poverty because her parents were frequently unemployed. During her formative years, she even spent a year in a shelter.

Her parents divorced because of her father’s alcoholism, but her mother continued her studies and worked in the evenings and on weekends. Maria was already involved in sports like football and athletics at a young age.

Maria Ohisalo earned a Bachelor’s degree in 2004 from Mäkelänrinne Sports High School, followed by a Master’s degree in Social Science from the University of Helsinki in 2011. She earned her Doctorate in Sociology from the Department of Social Sciences at the University of Eastern Finland in 2017.
