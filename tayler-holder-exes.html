Who Are Tayler Holder's Exes? Who Is the TikTok Star Dating?

TikTok star Tayler Holder has dated a few top names on the app. We take a look at his list of exes, along with details on his current girlfriend.

The gist:

  • Tayler Holder rose to fame as the star of the Brat series Dirt in 2018 and went on to amass millions of TikTok followers.
  • Tayler has dated Sommer Ray, Charly Jordan, and more.
  • In September 2023, Tayler introduced a new girlfriend.

TikTok star and former Hype House member Tayler Holder has been no stranger to sharing the details of his life online. He first rose to fame as the star of the Brat series Dirt in 2018, and he's since amassed millions of TikTok followers.

The actor-model-musician has been linked to a few TikTok sensations since he became a viral star on the app.

Who are Tayler Holder's exes? Keep on reading to find out which top creators he dated in the past, and who he's currently in a relationship with.

Who are Tayler Holder's exes?

The influencer confirmed that he was dating Team 10 member Kaylyn Slevin in the spring of 2019. Tayler revealed that the two had broken up in a YouTube video from August of 2019.

"I don't hate Kaylyn at all. We're still friends and we're going to continue to hang out. I'm going to support her in everything that she does. She's amazing, like all around," he said. He then explained that Kaylyn was busy, which had led to their split.

"With everything going on in her life right now, she's just not ready for a relationship. I respect that because she was very honest and came to me and told me right away," he continued.

Before he dated Kaylyn, Tayler teased that he was interested in pursuing Logan Paul's former assistant, Ayla Woodruff. He jokingly asked her to be his girlfriend in a Mukbang video he uploaded in February of 2019.

Article continues below advertisement

Following the end of his relationship with Kaylyn, Tayler teased a romance with Dixie D'Amelio. The two frequently posted TikTok content together toward the end of 2019 (they were both in the Hype House at that time), and many fans expected one of them to post about their relationship on Valentine's Day. 

The speculation was never confirmed, and Dixie moved on with her Attaway General co-star Griffin Johnson.

For several years, Tayler also taunted fans on YouTube with a potential relationship with his best friend, Kelianne Stankus. While it does not appear as if the two did date, they did kiss for the camera in a bid to get more views in a 2017 video. They also pretended to be a couple for another video in June of 2020, but Kelianne went on to be in a relationship with model Chase Mattson.

While Tayler's list of exes consisted of fellow internet stars, he then went on to date a new woman who was also quite familiar with the public eye. Beginning in May of 2020, fans started suspecting that Tayler and model Sommer Ray were dating. Tayler posted a TikTok video about being "booed up" and he tagged Sommer. In the TikTok, he also wrote "5.19.20," which appears to be the date that they began seeing one another.

Shortly before her relationship with Tayler began, Sommer was rumored to be with Machine Gun Kelly.

Fellow TikTok star Bryce Hall later essentially confirmed that Tayler and Sommer were dating during a livestream on the app. "It looks like they are," Bryce said when asked if Sommer and Tayler were an item. "They're definitely feeling all up on each other's faces and stuff." He later joked that they were "definitely, maybe... kissing. I will say that they have probably kissed."

After Sommer tweeted about being disrespected on June 16, 2020, many of her fans assumed that she was referring to Tayler, and that the two had broken up. "Your apology needs to be as loud as your disrespect was," she wrote in the cryptic message.

At the time, it was reported that the two had unfollowed one another on Instagram.

If the couple did split, then their breakup was short-lived as they began posting PDA-filled photos and videos together again soon after. They were also spotted out in public together less than a week after Sommer posted her tweet. But, the pair ended their romance for good in July 2020.

Tayler went on to date model Charly Jordan from October 2020 to late 2021. They were on and off for a bit, making a YouTube video together announcing their breakup in March 2021 before reuniting that July and then ending things for good later that year. In their March breakup video, they'd said they were ending things amicably and that it was simply a matter of bad timing.

After his breakup with Charly, Tayler appeared to be focused on his music for a while. But by September 2023, he had a new lady love to announce...

Tayler introduced new girlfriend Drew Knotts in September 2023.

She has a large following on social media, and the pair appear smitten. Tayler's fans flooded the comments section of his video to wish the couple well.
