Things You Need to Know About Rapper DreamDoll

Born in The Bronx, New York City, DreamDoll, whose real name is Tabatha Robinson, is a rapper, model, and reality television personality. 

She rose to prominence after appearing in the reality television series Bad Girls Club. She was also a cast member on season eight of Love & Hip Hop: New York. Both reality TV shows documented the rapper's personal and professional life.

Below is everything you need to know about DreamDoll!

Dreamdoll Was Raised by a Single Mother

DreamDoll, who is presently 30 years old, had a humble beginning while growing up together with her two brothers and two sisters in The Bronx.

Speaking with Complex in November 2021, the diva revealed that she struggled in the early days of her life. That was because her father was in jail [for undisclosed reasons] while her single mother worked hard to feed the rapper and her four siblings.

Rapper DreamDoll striking a pose for a promotional post for a clothing brand. (Photo: Instagram)

"So when I was in elementary school, we had career day, where we wrote what we wanted to be when we grow up. And most kids would say — firefighter, police officer, teacher, and I wrote I wanted to be a star," she recalled dreaming of becoming a star as a kid.

"I didn't know how I was going to be a star, how I was going to come about, but I knew what I wanted to do."

At the time of the interview, the rapper also felt she would have been more structured if she had her dad in her life. Yet, she believed that she could become the person everyone dreams of becoming if she continued her good work.

DreamDoll Came Out as Bisexual in 2021

DreamDoll identified herself as bisexual when she decided to come out of the closet in June 2021.

She took to Instagram to open up about her sexuality, sharing a skin-baring, rainbow-colored picture.

"Happy Pride Month," she captioned her Pride-themed photo announcement. "I’m proud to announce that I'm bisexual."

Immediately after the Love & Hip Hop alum made the post, fans flooded her comment section with love and support.

"I knew it - was just waiting for u to accounts it - Proud of u," one commenter wrote while another added, "We're happy for you, sis!!"

She later thanked her fans for all the love and support via Twitter, writing, "I’ve been getting so much love and comfort in my dms. I love it here."

DreamDoll’s Dating Life Is Always a Topic of Discussion

Ever since DreamDoll came to the spotlight, her dating life has always been a topic of discussion among her fans. They are curious to know whom she is dating and how she is doing in her relationship.

However, DreamDoll is also not giving away the details about her personal life easily. Due to that, her present relationship status remains unknown.

But in the past, she dated fellow rapper YBN Almighty Jay, who she labeled as a toxic person sometime after their break up in 2019.

In an interview with popular YouTube blogger BLovesLife, she revealed that she broke up with her ex-boyfriend because she realized her worth and he was not treating her well.

DreamDoll was then linked to Damson Idris in 2021 after they were clicked together on the red carpet of the Academy Awards after-party. But they later clarified that they were not together.
