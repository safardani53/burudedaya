Kardashian &amp; Jenner Sisters In Blazer Outfits See Pics Hollywood Life

Kim Kardashian rocked an oversized black blazer with no bra and no pants to the Alexander Wang show during New York Fashion Week.

Blazers and KarJenners go hand-in-hand. As the pioneers for most modern trends, the reality television queens know how to make the menswear piece look like it came straight off the runway — not straight out of a dad’s closet. The ladies love to have a black or lacy bra peek out from underneath an oversized blazer, or accentuate their tiny waists with a cinched blazer.

And then there’s the classic blazer dress, which is truly multifunctional — Kylie Jenner wore a white one to celebrate New Year’s Eve 2018, while Kendall Jenner layered an orange Blaze Milano blazer over stockings for New York Fashion Week. Some blazers can even be worn for that no-bra look, as Kendall also demonstrated (twice in this roundup!) via the clever use of boob tape.
