Who is Nella Rose? | The Sun

NELLA Rose shot to fame as a social media star, before making the switch to television.

The Influencer, is best known for YouTube, and now I'm A Celebrity.

Who is Nella Rose?

Nella Rose is an influencer, known for her beauty videos and comedic sketches.

She was born on July 20, 1997 in Belgium, and grew up there before moving with her family to London when she was seven-years-old.

Nella started making her mark in the industry in 2018 and has since risen to the top.

The star's witty and bubbly personality has seen her land TV hosting roles and her own popular YouTube series.

Sadly, Nella revealed that her parents died within four years of each other, with her mum passing in 2016, followed by her dad in 2020.

What memes is Nella Rose known for?

Nella has become a walking meme throughout her rise to fame.

You may recognise her from the viral video where she says "Are you not embarrassed?" where she stretches out the end of the word.

She says: "Are you not ashamed of yourself? Are you not embarrassed? This is really embarrassing."

What are Nella Rose's best YouTube videos?

Nella's most popular YouTube videos tend to be ones where she has collaboration with others.

For example, her Things That Girls Do That Boys Don't Like and Growing Up In An African Household have received the most views.

The TV personality is also known for her YouTube series The Pink Courtroom which aired in 2023.

It looks at the fallouts from toxic relationships, cheating and other dramas.

She also hosted Catfish UK in 2021, exposing liars on the internet alongside co-star Oobah Butler.

The star has amassed a huge fan base with her social media antics, including frequent collaborations with internet sensations Chunkz and Yung Filly.

Her down-to-earth brand has seen her following grow to a whopping 750,000 YouTube subscribers and 81 million views on her account.

Meanwhile, on Instagram she has garnered a huge fanbase of nearly 900,000 followers.

The influencer has collaborated with popular fashion brand Pretty Little Thing, and became an ambassador for industry giant, Fashion Nova’s Curve range.

The content creator launched her own unisex streetwear brand called Faces London in 2020, although the clothing no longer appears to be available.

Is Nella Rose married?

Nella Rose is not married.

She tends to keep her love life to herself but it's thought she is currently single.

Nella also does not have any children.

When did Nella Rose going on I'm A Celebrity?

Nella is part of the I'm A Celebrity 2023 line-up, and is the youngest campmate.

She entered camp on November 19, 2023, alongside other stars like Britney Spears’ sister Jamie Lynn, and Hollyoaks actor Nick Pickard

Speaking going into the jungle, Nella said: "My family have been laughing hysterically ever since I told them I was taking part.

"And they asked me why I agreed to do this but I’ve been watching I’m A Celebrity ever since I was a child and I still remember Ian Wright locked up in a room with an Ostrich.

"It’s so iconic and it’s why I said ‘yes’ immediately."

She then added: “I am terrified but I’m trying to think of the bigger picture.

"In this life, you have to face up to your fears. Yes I might scream the whole time but I am going to think about feeding human beings and I am going to try my best."
