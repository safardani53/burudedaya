Jersey Shore star Pauly D claims sex tapes of the cast exist in secret footage only in the hands of

PAULY "DJ Pauly D" DelVecchio says sex tapes of some of the wild adventures inside the Jersey Shore home exist.

And someone at MTV likely has them.

During an appearance the Full Send podcast, the Jersey Shore alum admitted cameras were always rolling inside the famous beach house, recording everything.

Even the casts most intimate moments.

"They have that footage somewhere. That goes in a safe somewhere," the 41-year-old DJ revealed on the podcast.

"Those are our sex tapes. There's a lot of them. I want to sell those some day."

Pauly said the cameras were always rolling - on everything - even if the dirtiest of the footage could never air on the cable network.

He explained: "We'd bring these girls home or whatever, and then all they can actually film is, like, all right the covers going on over us, so you know what happens."

While what comes next may never air, the footage apparently lives on somewhere.

"Those tapes exist. We always joke about it," the reality star confessed. "We're like, 'Yo, where is that footage?'"

NEW RELATIONSHIPS

These days, Pauly is a one-woman man.

The Rhode Island native is currently dating 29-year-old social media influencer Nikki Hall. 

The two met on the MTV reality show Double Shot at Love in 2019, and appeared together on the most recent season of Jersey Shore Family Vacation.

And it seems some of Pauly's pals are jealous of their steady relationship

During an episode that aired in July, Angelina Pivarnick touched on the problems in her marriage while getting drinks with DJ Pauly D’s girlfriend. 

In a confessional, Angelina said: “We’re always going at each other. 

“The resentment is there, but the now the non-trust is there, as well now because he moved out.” 

PAULY'S POSITIVE ROMANCE

After telling Nikki her relationship with Pauly is “so different and good,” Angelina continued: “It’s better to keep everything between you two.” 

She then slammed Chris for “running to social media” and complained that he posted a photo on Instagram of them, but covered her body by writing he was “living my best life.” 

Back in the confessional, Angelina said: “We have a lot of work to do when we get home. A lot.” 

In an earlier episode of Jersey Shore, Angelina admitted she wished her relationship was “like Pauly D and girlfriend Nikki’s” romance.  

She said in a confessional: “It’s nice to see that Nikki and Pauly have that where they can get into fights in their own home and not take it out of their home and show everyone in the world.
