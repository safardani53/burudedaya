Booze Bender in Aspen Just One Month After Welcoming New Baby?

He’s back.

After reportedly swearing off booze following the birth of Reign Aston Disick, Scott Disick apparently fell off the wagon hard during a recent trip to Aspen.

Sources say Scott took his boys (including famed graffiti artist and Harry Styles lookalike Alec Monopoly, below) to the ritzy Colorado ski town to blow off some steam after a month of baby duty…and things quickly got out of hand.

A member of Scott’s entourage allegedly hopped over the hotel bar to steal a bottle of Johnny Walker Black Label soon after they arrived, and that was just the beginning of the boozing.

TMZ reports that Scott racked up a four-figure bar tab during just two days in Aspen. 

The first night, Scott and his crew reportedly downed $840 worth of booze at the hotel bar. The next evening they stayed in, and ordered $600 worth of champagne and whiskey up to their room.

After they left, it saw discovered that their suite was littered with empty bottles and cigarette butts, resulting in a $500 cleaning bill.

So it sounds like Scott still knows how to party despite having just welcomed his third child.

Normally, that wouldn’t be a problem, but if you watch Kourtney and Khloe Take the Hamptons online, you know Scott’s alcoholism has been an ongoing issue. 

Disick was hospitalized for alcohol poisoning over the summer, and despite ditching rehab after just one day, he swore to his baby mama Kourtney that he lay off the booze for good.

Based on the news out of Aspen, we’re guessing that’s a promise that Scott wasn’t able to keep.
