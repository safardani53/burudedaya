The Real Housewives Of New Jersey Season 9 Reunion Sneak Peek Is Here!

The Real Housewives of New Jersey season nine reunion trailer is here – and we are so ready for the action! 

The secrets, finger-pointing, tears, and vicious attacks are all in the first look at what’s to come. 

In the trailer, Teresa Giudice, Melissa Gorga, Margaret Josephs, Dolores Catania, Jennifer Alydin and Jacki Goldschneider all go at it, holding nothing back while evaluating last season’s drama.

"You f-king coldhearted bitch," Teresa screams at (probably) sister-in-law Melissa.

But the main conflict appears to be among Teresa, Jackie, Margaret, and Jennifer, according to E!. 

More extreme yet totally impressive attacks get made throughout the sneak peek. 

"I lose brain cells talking to you," Jackie tells Teresa. 

It’s ok if you laughed out loud, that is priceless.  

And at one point, Teresa calls Jackie a "white trash bitch," and Jackie fights back with, "You’re not going to win a war of words with me."

"Really, bitch? You want to make a bet?" Teresa says. 

Jackie agrees to the bet.

But come on, ours is totally on Teresa. She craves blood. 

Teresa then makes a move that looks as though things are about to get physical.

And by the look of everyone’s faces, it probably does.

Things start getting emotional as host Andy Cohen begins asking the hard questions about her future with husband Joe Giudice, and his possible deportation.  

"What you’re saying is that you will go your separate ways," Cohen asks Teresa… who begins to break down in front of the camera. 

E! has learned that Teresa did, in fact, admit that she and Joe would go their separate ways should he be deported to Italy. 

But during a previous reunion, Teresa did say she would consider moving overseas. "I mean, Italy is a beautiful place to live."

And I’m sure a lot of the RHONJ castmates wouldn’t mind that.

Just make sure to get a visa, girl! 

Things take a comic turn, probably for the better, as the husbands are invited to join the conversation. 

And at one weird point, Joe Gorga even asks his sister if she is using a vibrator?!

Part one of the reunion airs on Wednesday, February 20 and features Margaret and Jennifer’s argument over words. 

Then, Jackie gets a turn in the hot seat while Teresa has some fun questioning her intentions and telling her she will never forgive her for what she said about her husband earlier in the season.

Which caused her to completely lose her mind and flip a table. Good stuff. 

Part two follows on Wednesday, February 27 and the fighting obviously continues. Teresa ultimately opens up about Joe’s likely deportation.

Leading to the final third part which airs Wednesday, March 6 where Joe, Danielle and Margaret rehash their overplayed feud. 

We are now prepared and so ready to watch all the mayhem unfold.
