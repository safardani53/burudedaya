Drivers are only just realising how to open car windows quickly - it could save your life

AN EXPERT has shared the little known feature that opens car windows quickly - it could save your life.

Drivers were stunned when they discovered this simple hack to make life easier on the road.

In a helpful video shared on TikTok by @dongchexiaoshimei, the glamorous car guru demonstrated some hidden functions.

She told her followers: "First raise the glass to the top.

"Then hook the window lift button for five seconds and the one key window lift function is set.

"In the future, when you want to lift the glass, you don't have to hold the button the whole time, you only need to press it once.

"Second, after getting out of the car and locking the car, press and hold the lock button of the car key for three seconds.

"All the windows will be closed automatically.

"Third, press and hold the unlock button of the car key for three seconds, all the windows will automatically drop."

This comes as other motor experts shared other tips and tricks which left drivers amazed.
