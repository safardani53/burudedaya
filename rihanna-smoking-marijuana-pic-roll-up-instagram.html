[PIC] Rihanna Smoking Marijuana Posts Instagram Pic With Roll-Up Hollywood Life

Rihanna’s Love Of Smoking

Rihanna is no stranger to a roll-up, and most recently she was seen smoking a lot of them while home in Barbados before Christmas. And last year, she celebrated the weed smoker’s holiday on April 20 by posting pics of her smoking a massive rolled-up “cigarette.” She even displays her love of pot on her clothing!

Rihanna posted this picture on Jan. 23, with the caption: “You a real [expletive], then ph**k wit me! #GOLD.” Is she dissing her on and off again lover Chris Brown?

Rihanna and Chris have come under fire a lot lately, and one person who disapproves of their relationship is Girls creator and star Lena Dunham, who also called out Rihanna for her love of smoking weed.

Lena Dunham Disses Rihanna

Lena spoke out very vocally to Alec Baldwin about how she doesn’t think Rihanna takes her status as a role model seriously.

“[Being a role model] is amazing. It’s an amazing thing and it’s like, it’s a platform that you have to take seriously,” Lena said. “Which is why sometimes it’s like I used to be really into Rihanna, that pop star, and then it’s like again, I don’t want to ever throw stones from my glass house, but I follow her on Instagram and I just think about how many little girls beyond what I could even comprehend are obsessed with Rihanna. Like you know, she left Barbados, she’s had this amazing career, she’s won a Grammy.”

“She’s talented. And then she gets back together with Chris Brown and posts a million pictures of them smoking marijuana together on a bed. And it cracks my heart in half in a way that makes me feel like I’m 95 years old,” the recent Golden Globe winner concluded.

She has a point! Rihanna does post a lot of pictures of herself smoking weed, and her relationship with Chris is less than perfect, even if they are making great progress.
