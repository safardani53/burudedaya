Liam Hemsworth and Miley Cyrus Just Got Married. For Real.

Miley Cyrus recently referred to Liam Hemsworth as her survival partner.

But now it appears as if the singer can maybe call her very handsome significant other by another name instead:

HUSBAND.

We’re serious this time.

We know you’ve heard rumors forever and ever and ever about Miley and Liam being married, but it appears this time around like those rumors truly are a reality.

How else to explain the series of Instagram photos that just went viral across the Internet?

A number of Photos and video went up today on surfer friend Conrad Jack Carr’s social media page — and they depict Cyrus and Hemsworth all dressed up, seemingly cutting a wedding cake.

You can check out that image above.

Below, meanwhile, you can see a singer belting out some kind of song in front of Mr. and Mrs. balloons.

Elsehwere in this array of intriguing snapshots, Liam and his brother Chris and Luke are doing a “shotski," which entails taking sips of alcohol off off a ski.

Look hard enough and you’ll be able to see Miley’s mother, Tish, in one of these pictures.

The photos were all snapped at Liam and Miley’s Nashville home, multiple outlets have confirmed.

Although Miley’s sisters, Noah and Brandi, aren’t seen in any of these images, they seemingly attended and posed together in a picture posted to Brandi’s Imstagram Story.

Noah, for her part, snapped a picture  with Carr and also shared a Story of herself with tears in her eyes and gave the camera a thumbs up.

This really does appear to be the real thing, folks.

Miley and Liam met in 2010 on the set of the Nicholas Sparks movie, The Last Song. 

They got engaged in 2012, but broke up 2014.

The stars reconciled the next year and got engaged again, yet they never seemed to be in any rush to tie the knot.

A week ago, Cyrus made a few hilarious headlines when she gave Hemsworth credit for his good dick game.

Yes, his good dick game.

"I call him my survival partner now," she had previously told Howard Stern, adding this month:

"He thinks it’s not romantic, but I learned that it is. It is why you pair up with someone is for survival."

We love it.
