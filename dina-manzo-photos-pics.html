Pics Of The Former RHONJ Star Hollywood Life

Dina Manzo pictured at The Real Housewives of New Jersey White Party at the Woodbury Country Club. 

Dina Cantin (previously Manzo) born March 7, 1972, in Brooklyn, New York, is an American television personality and entrepreneur. She’s best known for appearing on Bravo’s The Real Housewives of New Jersey, which led to her own series, Dina’s Party. The latter documented her party-planning business. Dina was a full-time cast member on Seasons 1 and 2 of The Real Housewives of New Jersey throughout 2009 and 2010. She’s the youngest of 11 children, including former RHONJ star Caroline Manzo. Dina was previously married to Thomas Manzo, who she shares a daughter, Lexi (born in 1996) with. They split in October 2012 and their divorce was finalized nearly four years later. Dina began dating entrepreneur David Cantin in 2015. They married in June of 2017, and Dina confirmed the union on social media in May of 2019. Following a 2017 home invasion, Dina and David relocated from New Jersey to the West Coast. On June 30, 2020, the U.S. Attorney’s Office in New Jersey released a press release and indictment charging Dina’s ex-husband, Thomas Manzo with conspiring to assault David Cantin in the spring of 2015.
