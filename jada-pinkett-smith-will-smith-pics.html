Jada Pinkett Smith &amp; Will Smith: Photos Hollywood Life

Will and Jada’s relationship has been in the spotlight for decades. See photos of them through the years here.

Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith met in 1994 on the set of ‘The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air’ when Jada was, funnily enough, trying out to be Will’s on-air girlfriend! The couple married in 1997, and have two children together, Jaden Christopher Smith and Willow Camille Reign Smith. Will has another child, Trey, from his marriage with Sheree Zampino before he was with Jada. Both Will and Jada have opened up about their relationship struggles on her talk show, ‘Red Table Talk,’ on Facebook. In 2022, Will made waves after he slapped Oscar presenter Chris Rock over a joke he made about Jada’s hair loss.
