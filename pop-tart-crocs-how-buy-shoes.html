Pop-Tart Crocs: How can I buy the limited-edition shoes?

THE BELOVED shoe brand Crocs is partnering with Kellogg's Pop-Tart for a new limited-edition release.

Now that consumers have seen what the shoes look like, they want to know how they can get their hands on a pair.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

What are the limited-edition Pop-Tart Crocs?

On August 8, 2023, Crocs announced the addition of a new Pop-Tart-themed shoe that is designed to bring fashion and flavor together.

"Pop-Tarts has been committed to inventive snacking since we first turned toast and jam into an iconic, irresistible toaster pastry, and our latest ingenious collab further lets fans get creative and express themselves while challenging the idea of where Pop-Tarts can show up, and what can show up on a Pop-Tart toaster pastry," Heidi Ray, Pop-Tarts' Senior Director of Marketing, said in a press release.

"When thinking about bringing these two iconic and beloved brands together, we couldn't help but notice the uncanny similarity of the Crocs shoes and Unfrosted Pop-Tarts. We're excited to unveil our newest snacking twist, an interactive experience where fans can add flavor to both using fun and distinct charms."

The shoe is off-white and comes with Pop-Tart-themed Jibbitz.

Where can I buy the shoes?

Unfortionely, the limited-edition Pop-Tart Crocs will not be available for purchase at a retail location.

They will, however, be available for purchase on croc-tarts.com beginning August 9, 2023, at 12pm EST.

Additional drops will then occur on August 11, August 16, and August 18 at the same time.

Sixty kits will be up for grabs during each drawing and are only available while supplies last.

Do the Crocs have edible Jibbitz?

Along with dropping a limited-edition shoe, Cros and Pop-Tarts are also releasing edible Jibbitz.

Each purchase of the limited-edition Pop-Tart Crocs will come with the shoes as well as a "box of Unfrosted Strawberry Pop-Tarts and Crocs-shaped strawberry and blueberry-flavored gummy Jibbitz charms with edible glue to decorate (and eat!) your unfrosted Pop-Tarts."
