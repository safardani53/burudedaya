Emmerdales Eden Taylor-Draper wows in see-through top as cast look nothing like their characters

EDEN Taylor-Draper wowed as she arrived at the Emmerdale summer party in a see-through top.

The 25-year-old, who plays Belle Dingle in Emmerdale, looked worlds away from the Dales in the purple top which was cropped to show a hint of her toned tummy.

Blonde beauty Eden teamed it with some light blue jeans and heels.

Eden joined Emmerdale as Belle in 2005 and became the third actress to play the character.

She was snapped arrival at The Ivy in Leeds with Victoria Sugden actress Isabel Hodgins.

Isabel, 29, looked summery in a floral print top and white trousers.

Also in attendance was Daisy Campbell, who stars as Amelia Spencer in the ITV soap.

Daisy, 19, opted for an oversized blazer which hid most of the rest of her outfit.

Amy Walsh - sister of Girls Aloud star Kimberley - looked incredible in all white as she stepped out for the do.

Meanwhile Michelle Hardwick, who's played Vanessa Woodfield since 2012, turned up the glamour in a long leopard print dress.

Finishing the party line-up were Caleb Dingle actor William Ash, Chris Chittell, who stars as Eric Pollard and Chris Bisson, who plays Jai Sharma.
