Pics Of The Hottest Hunks Then &amp; Now Hollywood Life

Like sands through the hourglass, so are the Days of Our Lives… hottest hunks! Through the soap opera’s 55-year (and 55 season) run, there have been plenty of devastatingly handsome actors to delight and entertain viewers. And maybe, just a few famous men you didn’t realize used to be on the show! 

Take Jensen Ackles, for example. Diehard fans know Jensen as Dean Winchester on Supernatural, a character he’s played for the past 15 years. But before that, he was Eric Brady on Days for three years! The sweet character loved helping people — and loved some gorgeous women, while he was at it. And then, there’s Chandler Massey. The actor is the first to win a Daytime Emmy for playing a gay character, and he certainly deserved it. He’s one half of WilSon, after all.
