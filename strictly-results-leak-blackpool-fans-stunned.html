Strictly fans stunned as Blackpool week results leak online after 'fix' row

STRICTLY Come Dancing fans are furious yet again after the results leaked online as the show is embroiled in a fix row.

The celebrity ballroom dancing competition airs its main episode live each Saturday night but pre-records Sunday night’s results show straight after.

This has meant that every single week the result has leaked online before fans have the chance to watch the results show - ruining the outcome for them.

And this week it has happened again.

The Sun will not reveal the results of the leak in an effort to preserve the surprise for fans.

However, those who have seen the spoiler have made their fury known, as the show has been embroiled in a fix row over former Come Dancing host Angela Rippon surviving to this week's Blackpool show - 40 years after she hosted there.

One wrote: "Wow...we finally get a genuine shock!"

Another added: "I'm horrified at who was in the dance off !

"Right result IMHO but they should never have been there in the first place."

In last night’s episode Waterloo Road star Layton Williams and partner Nikita Kuzmin topped the leaderboard, alongside former Coronation Street star Ellie Leach and her partner Vito Coppola.

Layton and Nikita were praised for their pole-based routine to Ain't No Other Man by Christina Aguilera during the coveted Blackpool week.

The pair were placed joint top of the leaderboard, but were unable to secure full marks.

Three of the judges awarded the pair a 10 for their couple's choice routine, while Craig Revel Horwood awarded the couple a nine, resulting in an overall judges' score of 39.

Before they revealed their scores, Revel Horwood told Williams: "This is a little bit of a dilemma for me because I loved it, number one, but it wasn't entirely in sync."

He went on: "That being said, you are one of the most incredible dancers that I have seen on this show over the 20 years I've been doing it."

Strictly head judge Shirley Ballas complimented the actor and said: "Well you know, for me, it takes a lot, really, to impress me, but this really is creative genius."

At the other end of the leaderboard, Angela Rippon and partner Kai Widdrington achieved just 28 points.
