Primark has launched ANOTHER range of Friends PJs and for 6 we think they might be their cutest

COULD we be any more obsessed with this adorable Friends pyjama set from Primark?

The super sweet vest and short set, costing just £6, has just hit the shelves and we absolutely love it.

The sweet top advises us to: "Dress like Rachel, cook like Monica and believe like Phoebe."

Meanwhile, the shorts feature tinyCentral Perk motifs.

On Primark's Insta page, people love the new jimjams.

phoebemlx wrote: "Omg. So cringe I need it @immiehart"

This isn't the first time this year Primark has absolutely charmed us with its Friends-themed nightwear.

In April we revealed the shop was selling a fab Friends-patterned tee and trackie set for just £10.
