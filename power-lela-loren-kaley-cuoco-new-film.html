Power star Lela Loren posts sneak peek at new film with Kaley Cuoco - but fans are completely distra

POWER fans have demanded Angela Valdez actress Lela Loren reunites with lover Ghost in heaven - as she posted a cryptic shot of her new movie with The Big Bang Theory star Kaley Cuoco.

Lela, who played James St Patrick's - known as Ghost - love interest who was brutally gunned down by Tommy Egan in the hit Netflix and Starz show, received backlash when she excitedly posted a shot on the set of her new movie on Instagram.

The photo shows Lela in a pink suit near director Patrick Hughes, with comedian Kevin Hart grimacing in the foreground.

Lela wrote: “Comment on what you all think we are checking out. #manfromtoronto”

One fan said: "Ghost pulling up to heaven to greet you anddddd Raina.”

A second added: “Angela Valdez! I miss you.”

A third said :“Ghost just got out of the car for his girl.”

A fourth  said: “Tommy pulling up on Angie."

Another, referring to her co-star Kevin Hart in her picture, chimed  in: “Is that Tommy’s car? Why is James St Patrick so small? .”

Lela will star in Man From Toronto alongside The Big Bang Theory star Kaley Cuoco.

But fans aren’t happy about her moving on from Power. They want both her and Ghost to return.

Instead of celebrating her new role, they took over her Instagram and begged her to return to Power and recreate some of her steamy sex scenes with star of the show Ghost, played by Omari Hardwick.

Just last month viewers went crazy when Lela wrote on Instagram: “So thrilled to be part of this team. #manfromtoronto”

One wrote: “I just want you back on Power :( “A second wrote: “Hopefully you make a love scene in there. Lol.”
