But her bargain TikTok Shop find - a hot water belt - is now her "favourite thing" as it keeps the cold at bay.

Showing it off in a short TikTok video (@jessicabanktattoo), she said: "Does your man keep turning the heating down on car drives?

"Cosy hack for all you cold tatties like me.

"Let me introduce you to my best TikTok find. Hot water bottle belt with hand warmers."

She added: "I honestly take this everywhere. Hairdressers, car journeys, lash appointments and dog walks.

"It's my new favourite thing."

Tattoo artist Jessica picked up the koala bear hot water belt from TikTok Shop.

With snow on the way, you might be thinking about other ways to save money while trying to stay warm.

As energy prices remain high, one heating expert has shared his top tips on how to keep the cost down.

Rob Nezard, MD of UK Radiators, says you should always be getting your radiators and heating system regularly maintained, checked and repaired to keep initial costs down.

He said even reducing your thermostat by one degree can save you up to £145-a-year.

And it's bad news if you have pretty radiator covers throughout your home.
