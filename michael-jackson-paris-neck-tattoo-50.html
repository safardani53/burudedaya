Michael Jackson's wild daughter Paris, 22, shows off new neck tattoo as her ink count now exceeds 50

PARIS Jackson showed off her new neck tattoo as she stepped out in Los Angeles make-up free yesterday.

The 22-year-old was spotted leaving Tattoo Mania on the Sunset Strip after she got a new wrist inking as her tattoo count now exceeds 50.

The starlet was clad in an oversized black hoodie which she teamed with a pair of skinny jeans and sneakers with her hair styled into a loose bun.

The daughter of Michael Jackson got her intricate neck inking in March before lockdown, which was from one of the Lord of the Rings trilogy by J.R.R. Tolkien.

The crimson-colored calligraphic tatt read: "A light from the shadows [shall spring]".

But since then, Paris took to her Instagram yesterday to share her latest arm tattoo, which was a Latin phrase stating: "libera te tutemet ex inferis".

The phrase is a quote from sci-fi film Event Horizon's and means "save yourself from hell."

Last week, she also paid homage to her favorite rock band Manchester Orchestra with an arm tattoo.

The tattoo was a black ink drawing of a tree and a climber as featured on the cover of their album A Black Mile To The Surface, which was released in 2017.

Paris has forever memorialized the people and things that mean the most to her with 53 sentimental tattoos all over her body.

The model used the ink to tribute her favorite bands, artists, flowers, animals and of course, several that pay homage to her late father pop icon, Michael Jackson, and those closest to her.

The legendary musician’s only daughter got a tattoo in 2016 on the inside of her arm that’s a recreation of the cover for his famed 1991 album Dangerous.

Her tribute features a drawing of her dad’s eyes as they peer through a window above an elephant, ram and an assortment of other animals with a monkey raising a crown right above.

The word “Bad” is written with red ink in the same font as Michael’s hugely successful 1987 album of the same name on the inside of her hand.

She also honored her father with the word “Applehead” - a pet nickname she used to call him - written across her foot in his own handwriting.

Paris also paid tribute to her family with a white "B" for her brother Blanket on the inside of her ankle, the name of her goddaughter Isabella D'Amore Alt written on her arm and got a matching arm tattoo of a spoon with longtime family friend and godfather Macaulay Culkin, 39.

The animal lover inked her favorite creatures which include the name of her rescue dog “Koa” on her ankle, a wolf, a deer and two dragonflies on her forearm.

She also has one large tattoo of Frank the Rabbit - the anti-hero of the 2001 cult favorite Donnie Darko - with the words "wake up" from the film scribbled underneath.

The musician channeled her spiritual side with seven colorful chakras that represent energy points going down both her torso and her back as well as a massive abstract symbol that covers her right shoulder.

World-renowned artists she admires are grouped together on her forearm that include painters Pablo Picasso, Amedeo Modigliani and Jean-Michele Basquiat as well her latest new tattoo - a drawing of Vincent Van Gogh.

The singer - who formed a musical duo named The Soundflowers in 2018 - paid tribute to her favorite acts with four Led Zeppelin symbols from their 1971 album on her arm.

She has an image of a snake wrapped around a sword with wings that honors Motley Crue’s Dr. Feelgood album cover and even the word "Motley" inside of her lip.

Paris also eulogized John Lennon with his famous lyrics "imagine all the people living life in peace" from the song Imagine on her arm as well as a drawing of the beloved Beatle directly above.

Paris has sported several small accent pieces throughout her body which include a star on her hand, a circular symbol on her thumb, three tiny finger tattoos, a heart with an arrow on her forearm, one skull and one cactus on her ankle and a pair of little feathers behind her ear.

The fashionista also has a drawing of a tree on her ankle, a large sunflower on her forearm and a quote on her collarbone.

She also has one red Haitian Voduo symbol on her inner arm, the Millennium Falcon from Star Wars on her ankle, a symbol supporting the Standing Rock Indian Reservation on her inner ankle and "I Liked it Better When You Had No Heart" written across her ribcage.

Paris previously explained her love for getting tattoos on Instagram and said: "Some people like them, some people absolutely hate them. I appreciate art, I always have.

"Especially when that art means something to me. Today I can look at my inner forearms and see art that has meaning for me, I don't see a dark past anymore.

Adding: "My scars and past of self-hatred have been covered by loving marks, creativity, ingenuity… and depth.

"Sure I will always carry my past with me, but I see ink as a way of changing for the better, always improving."

In total, the entertainer has more than 50 tattoos across her body and that count will likely go up as Paris has expressed her desire to add to her extensive collection in the future.
