GMA's Robin Roberts shares sweet picture from time away from studio - but fans are all distracted by

Good Morning America's Robin Roberts' recent Instagram post has caught the attention of fans for a giant reason.

Robin Roberts shared a snap of her time away in Paris, but fans couldn’t help but comment on a guest who appeared in her Instagram post.

Roberts shared a post of her spending time with French basketballer Victor Wembanyama yesterday.

Fans and followers flooded the post's comments section about Wembanyama’s height.

The 19-year-old is a staggering 7’ 2” tall and towered over the 5’ 10” presenter.

Roberts added that this meeting with Wembanyama will be aired on Good Morning America soon.

Wembanyama is a French professional basketball player with the Metropolitans 92 in the French LNB Pro A, the country's top-tier men’s professional basketball league.
