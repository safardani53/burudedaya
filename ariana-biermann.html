Ariana Biermann Was Arrested For Allegedly Drinking And Driving

Ariana Biermann, the daughter of reality star Kim Zolciak-Biermann, was detained at 1 a.m. on Saturday, August 13, for allegedly driving under the influence. According to TMZ, the 20-year-old was arrested at the Forsyth County Jail for “driving while intoxicated, improper/erratic lane changes, and underage possession/purchase of alcohol.” According to reports, the star youngster was freed on a $5,120 bail with a court date, but she disputes the claims and plans to fight them.

Kim Zolciak, Ariana’s mother, is well-known for her debut on The Real Housewives of Atlanta in 2008. Kim is married to Kroy Evan Biermann, a former American football player.

Ariana Biermann plans to defend her DUI accusations

Ariana Biermann’s team said that the influencer was involved in a “small fender collision” on Saturday and intends to contest the allegations. Their lawyer, Justin Spizman, stated:

“Ariana got engaged in a small car accident. When the police came, the investigating officer promptly launched a DUI inquiry. Despite Ariana’s repeated assertions that she was disturbed and nervous as a result of the small collision, the officer wrongly determined she was intoxicated.”

He continued:

“That was not true. She categorically rejects these claims and plans to defend them since she is not guilty of them.”

Ariana was reportedly joined by her on-again, off-again beau, Hudson McLeroy, at the time of the incident. Hudson was also charged with “driving under the influence, giving alcohol to a juvenile, and breaking the terms of his restricted driving license.” He was allegedly in the passenger seat when the authorities arrested them. Hudson has also been freed on bail, according to TMZ.

Who is Ariana Biermann?

Kim Zolciak-Biermann and her ex-husband, Daniel Toce, have a daughter named Ariana Biermann. She was born in Atlanta, Georgia on October 17, 2001. Ariana’s parents divorced in 2003, only two years after she was born. Kim split up with Daniel after discovering his involvement with a 15-year-old girl, according to several accounts. Daniel was subsequently charged and imprisoned for having an illicit connection with a minor. Kim later married football player Kroy Biermann, who adopted Ariana and her sister Brielle Biermann.

Ariana came to prominence after her mother’s appearance on The Real Housewives of Atlanta in 2008. She also starred in Don’t Be Tardy, a 2012 program based on her mother and family. Hudson McLeroy is her current boyfriend. When they were in high school, the pair started dating. The same year, Ariana uploaded a snapshot of them on her Instagram account. The pair were dressed up for their homecoming dance in October when the photo was taken. Ariana attended a private high school and intends to attend the Savannah College of Art and Design. In a March 2022 interview, she stated her intentions as follows:

“Right now, I’m majoring in business administration to expand my understanding of having my own firm — I aim to go [SCAD] for fashion next year.”

She is presently assisting her family’s cosmetics company, KAB Cosmetics. With over 656K Instagram followers, the young star has a sizable fan base. Her net worth is estimated to be approximately $550,000.
