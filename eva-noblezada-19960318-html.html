Eva Maria Noblezada is an American actress and singer best known for her work in Broadway theater and in independent films. She also played Eurydice in Hadestown on Broadway, for which she received a second Tony Award nomination for Best Actress in a Musical, as well as a 2020 Grammy Award for Best Musical Album.
