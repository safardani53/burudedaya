The 66 Best Beauty and Fashion Gifts of 2024

If you're finding yourself scrambling for gifts for the fashion or beauty lover in your life, then you've come to the right spot.

From our best-rated PEOPLE Tested makeup products to celebrity-approved Ugg slippers, we have all of the recommendations you'll need in order to take the brainwork out of last-minute shopping. Our picks have been hand-selected by PEOPLE editors to make sure you can cross off those last few names on your gift list.

Keep reading to unwrap the best beauty and fashion gifts to give to the special people in your life.

Best Gifts Under $10

Baimei Gua Sha & Jade Roller Facial Tools

Give the gift of at-home facial massage with the Baimei Gua Sha & Jade Roller Facial Tools from Amazon. This skincare set comes with a jade roller and gua sha tool designed to massage, sculpt, and de-puff the complexion, and it’s currently on sale for $10. From budget-friendly stocking stuffers to small gift ideas for friends, you can’t go wrong with a jade roller and gua sha tool set. 

Latme Ice Roller

The Latme Ice Roller is another excellent beauty gift idea under $10. The ice roller is designed to not only massage the face but also de-puff and calm the skin, improve circulation, and improve overall radiance. On top of that, you can use it to relieve migraines, too.   

Samnyte Hair Wax Stick

TikTok swears by wax sticks to get the slicked back look without the crunchiness of hairspray. This wax stick from Amazon costs less than $10 and is formulated with castor oil which provides nourishment to strands and can even promote hair growth while taming flyaways and frizz. 

Charms Original Italian Charm Bracelet

Italian charm bracelets are back in style — and this starter one at Amazon is less than $10. The bracelet comes in dozens of colors including silver, gold, multi-color, and black, and there are lots of adorable charms available across the site for $6 each so you can start customizing.

Hilxury Silver Chunky Hoop Earrings Set

Silver is this season's trending accent, from metallic loafers to silver-toned jewelry. However you want to lean into it, it's hard to pass up this under-$10 chic earring set that comes with three sets of chunky earrings that are suitable for everyday wear — to the office, holiday parties, errands, and beyond.

SL Sursarbelor 4-Piece Hair Claw Clips

If you have even semi-long hair, odds are you need to be within arm's length of a claw clip at any given moment for a quick updo solution. Hair claw clips are not only easy, but you can let your personality shine through via different colors, shapes, and designs. We love the variety in this four pack that adds a stylish touch to your hair while doubling as a functional hair tool. This hair accessory simply works harder than any other we have in our arsenal.

Best Shoe Gifts 

Koolaburra by Ugg Advay Slip-On

Slippers are always a good gift idea and the Koolaburra by Ugg Advay Slip-Ons are one of our favorite styles. These slip-ons are versatile enough to wear as slippers, house shoes, or comfy booties to run errands in. Constructed from suede with a faux fur lining, the booties are available in chestnut, black, and beige. 

Ugg Classic Ultra Mini

UGG Classic Ultra Mini boots are another comfy shoe option to give this holiday season. Aleenla Smile Face Slippers

While slippers may be a predictable gift, that doesn't mean they still won't spark joy. We love these cute under-$15 slippers at Amazon that feature a cute smiley face design and come in eight different color options. While they're meant to be house shoes, they do have a rubber sole for some traction in case you do need to step outside to water the plants or grab the mail.

Hush Puppies Penny Loafers

The Hush Puppies Penny Loafers feature a timeless design and durable construction. Available wide and medium fits with an All-Day Comfort. Bounce footbed, these loafers are great for everyday wear and versatile enough to dress up or down, depending on the occasion. 

Merry People Billie Clogs

These Merry People Billie Clogs are constructed from durable and high-quality natural rubber with a neoprene lining and plenty of arch support for added comfort. The slip-on rain shoes are naturally waterproof and designed to keep your soles dry while adding a touch of comfort and style to your rainy day look.

The North Face ThermoBall Mules

Give the gift of cozy with The North Face ThermoBall Mules. Designed for cold weather, these North Face slippers feature ThermoBall Eco insulation and are constructed from a recycled P.E.T. ripstop upper with a water-repellent finish to keep your soles warm and dry. 

Merrell Bristol Hiker

For the outdoor enthusiast in your life, consider gifting the Merrell Bristol Hiker. These boots are made from recycled materials and designed with a FloatPro Foam midsole for comfort, plus Cleansport NXT to help keep odors at bay. 

Sorel Kinetic Breakthru Tech Lace Sneaker

The Sorel Kinetic Breakthru Tech Lace Sneaker is one of the most comfortable pairs we own and would make a great gift for those with athleisure style. Designed with eco-friendly materials, lots of cushion, and a molded rubber outsole with lots of traction, these sneakers are ultra-durable with long-lasting comfort.
