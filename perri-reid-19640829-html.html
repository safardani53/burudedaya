Perri Reid (Soul Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Perri Arlette Reid, known by her former stage name Pebbles, is an American singer-songwriter, businesswoman, record producer and musical director. Reid is perhaps best known for her hit songs from the late 1980s and early 1990s such as "Girlfriend", "Mercedes Boy" and "Giving You the Benefit". In addition to his recording career, Reid also founded the contemporary R&B group TLC. Reid is currently a pastor in Atlanta, known as "Sister Perri".
