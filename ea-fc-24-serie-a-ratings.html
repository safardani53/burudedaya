EA Sports FC 24 Serie A best player ratings revealed Maignan cedes the top spot

EA SPORTS FC 24 has just revealed the ratings of the best 24 football players from Italy’s Serie A. 

In the lead-up to its release on September 29, 2023, the video game has been announcing its player rankings for the world’s top football leagues.

Napoli striker Victor Osimhen tops the list with an overall rating of 88, which leaves Serie A outside the 90+ club.

The Nigerian striker is one of the most sought-after players in Europe and was just nominated for the Ballon d’Or award.

Nevertheless, previously revealed rankings for the Premier League, Spain’s La Liga, and Germany’s Bundesliga, all had the top players rated at or above 90.

Sharing the second place in Serie A are AC Milan goalie Mike Maignan and Inter Milan striker Lautaro Martínez, both rated 87 overall.

Maignan keeps his FIFA 23 rating unchanged, but loses the top spot in the Serie A ranking to Osimhen, who received a nice upgrade.

Moving down the list, we find Nicolò Barella, Rafael Leão, Paulo Dybala, Khvicha Kvaratskhelia, and Wojciech Szczęsny, all rated 86.

At 85, we can see six players, Inter Milan’s Bastoni and Çalhanoğlu, AC Milan’s Theo Hernández, Lazio’s Immobile, Di Lorenzo from Napoli, and Berardi playing for Sassuolo.

Roma’s latest addition, Romelu Lukaku, who is on loan from Chelsea, makes his debut with the Wolves rated 84.

England national team defender and ex-Chelsea star Fikayo Tomori, who now plays for AC Milan, has an overall rating of 84 too.

Former Manchester United defender Chris Smalling, who joined Roma in 2020, is another Englishman on the list, again rated 84.
