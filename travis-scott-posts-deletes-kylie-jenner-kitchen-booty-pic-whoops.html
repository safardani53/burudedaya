Travis Scott Posts, Deletes Kylie Jenner Kitchen Booty Pic: Whoops!

Travis Scott and Kylie Jenner have two children together.

Despite their high profile status, the couple have become increasingly private in recent years.

Travis shared a pic of Kylie’s booty while singing her praises.

Their pivot towards privacy may explain why he hastily deleted the unedited photo.

“Shawty in here throwing dat s–t down!” Travis Scott exclaimed on his Instagram Story.

The now-deleted photo showed Kylie Jenner from behind, unedited, presumably while preparing food.

Travis clearly intended to praise Kylie while also showing off her eye-catching posterior.

There is nothing unflattering about this image, from what we can see.

It looks like Kylie, who has enough money to avoid any kind of labor that she chooses, is cooking up a storm.

She looks great, the kitchen looks great (albeit in use), and Travis was praising the food.

Perhaps Travis deleted the photo on his own, or at Kylie’s request.

We could speculate about what may or may not have motivated the deletion.

But ultimately, no one needs an excuse to want a photo of themselves deleted — or never posted at all.

Sunday was Father’s Day.

In the Kardashian-Jenner clan, where family is everything, no one would forget.

Kylie posted a tribute to Travis, the father of her two children, with a rare family photo.

The picture shows Travis reclining in bed, chowing down on some ramen.

Snuggled up to him are 4-year-old daughter, Stormi Webster.

Travis is cuddling the couple’s second child, The Baby Formerly Known as Wolf.

Just in case anyone was unclear about the post’s meaning, Kylie spelled it out.

“Happy Father’s Day!” she exclaimed in the caption. “Daddy, we love you.”

Kylie obscured Baby #2’s face with a brown heart emoji.

After more than a decade of “mommy bloggers” and those detestable “family vlogs,” many parents are waking up.

The occasional photo of a family gathering or a cute kid is great.

Documenting your child’s every moment for thousands or millions of strangers is not.

Now, Kylie is taking steps to protect her children’s privacy.

That is no small feat for even a minor TV star, let alone a celebrity of her level of fame.

But making an effort to shield her children from haters and trolls and over exposure will give them more choices in life. It’s the right thing to do.
