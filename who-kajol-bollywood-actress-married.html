Who is Kajol and is she married?

KAJOL is an award-winning actress, who leads the way in Hindi cinema.

She has starred in the likes of Kuch Kuch Hota Hai, My Name Is Khan, and Fanaa.

Who is Kajol?

Kajol Devgan was born on August 5, 1974, and is known mononymously as Kajol.

Described as one of the most successful actresses of her time, she has won numerous awards, including six Filmfare Awards,

In 2011, Kajol was honoured with the Padma Shri by the Government of India.

She is the daughter of actress Tanuja, and producer Shomu Mukherjee.

Kajol made her acting debut with Bekhudi (1992) while still in school, but quit classes after her career took off.

In addition to acting in films, she is a social activist and known for her work with widows and children.

What has Kajol starred in?

Kajol has starred in many films which incldes the thriller Fanaa (2006), and the drama My Name Is Khan (2010).

Her highest-grossing releases came with the comedy Dilwale (2015) and the period film Tanhaji (2020).

Kajol also had success with the movies Tribhanga (2021), and The Trial (2023).

Is Kajol married?

Kajol met her future husband Ajay Devgn in 1995 whilst they were co-starring in Gundaraj.

When they first got together they were called "an unlikely pair" by the press, due to their different personalities.

In February 1999, after four years of dating they married in a traditional Maharashtrian Hindu ceremony at the Devgan family home.

Does Kajol have any children?

Together with her husband Ajay she has two children together.

The couple welcomed their daughter Nysa in 2003.

She was followed by their son, Yug, who was born in 2010.

What is Kajol's net worth?

As of 2023, Kajol has an estimated net worth of £23M.

This has been accumulated through her steady work of starring in Bollywood films.
