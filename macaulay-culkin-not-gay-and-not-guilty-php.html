Macaulay Culkin: ‘Not gay’ and ‘not guilty’

» More Celebs


Not only is Macaulay Culkin labeling himself “not gay,” but he also claiming “not guilty.” He entered the plea at an Oklahoma City court, where he appeared following charges of possessing Xanax without a prescription and carrying some hash. He’s set to return to the court house on Dec. 2, which will force his reps into an even more awkward position than his sexual orientation allegations: why, for fuck’s sake, was he in Oklahoma?
