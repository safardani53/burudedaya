I'd rather have another boob job than go back on SAS Who Dares Wins, I'm covered in scars for life,

AMBER Turner no doubt outlasted her fake nails on Celebrity SAS: Who Dares Wins.

But she’d still rather go back under the knife for a second boob job than suffer through the show.

The Only Way Is Essex star called it quits last night ahead of the gruelling kidnapping task dished out by DS Rudy Reyes and his crew.

And while she was “black and blue” with bruises, it was the thought of having a bag placed over her head that proved too much for Amber.

She explained: “I set myself a goal to get to that stage where they kidnap you. I was anticipating them putting a bag on my head, and that was honestly the only thing I was worried about.

“It sounds so silly because it was something so minor, but I’m claustrophobic and I don’t do well in close spaces, and I knew theft were going to try to kidnap us and put a bag on my head.

“I was just thinking, ‘I physically can’t, I’d rather give my armband before that happens’ so, I took my armband off.”

Amber added: “I would rather have my boobs done again and a facelift than go through the pain I went through - my body was in shock because I was in so much pain.

“I had jungle rot on my feet, and it was excruciating pain, I’ve never experienced pain like it.

“I’d lost loads of weight, I was black and blue and so bruised - I’m covered in scars for life. I looked like a broken woman, it’s like I’d come back from war - gaunt, like a skeleton, my teeth were popping out of my face.”
