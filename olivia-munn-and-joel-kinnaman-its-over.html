Olivia Munn and Joel Kinnaman: It's Over!

It is all over for Olivia Munn and Joel Kinnaman.

The couple of two years split a few months ago, sources have confirmed to Us Weekly, with an insider saying “distance” played a major role in the break-up.

“They ended things a few months ago, but both seem fine,” a mole tells the tabloid. “He’s back filming in Toronto and she’s now in L.A. for good.”

Kinnaman is best known for his role as Robocop and also for playing a detective on The Killing.

Munn, meanwhile, got her major break on The Daily Show and is now the best part of HBO’s The Newsroom.

As recently as February, the stars were seen together in New York, acting all sweet at a Knicks game. The actor then referred to Munn as a “hoot” and “a lot of fun” during an appearance on Today.

Munn’s dating history include Brad Richards, Justin Timberlake and Chris Pine, so we’re guessing she bounces back just fine in this case.
