Teen, 16, arrives at prom in a Rolls-Royce decorated with four MILLION Swarovski crystals that cost

A teenager stole the show at her school prom after arriving in a Rolls Royce - covered in more than four million Swarovski crystals.

Isha Sanah Akhtar, 16, made a jaw-dropping entrance to the end-of-year celebration on Thursday, July 4.

She also dressed to impress and donned a stunning blue and grey coloured gown to the prom which cost £260.

Isha enlisted the services of her own camera crew for £300 to capture her getting ready and travelling to the prom in the Rolls Royce Phantom.

The luxury touches to the vehicle were provided by Isha's uncle Amar Akbar, who travelled with her to the prom at Dunkenhalgh Hotel in Clayton-le-Moors near Accrington, Lancs.

Isha, who lives in Audley, Blackburn, with her mum Nabila Akhtar, had a night to remember after turning up in the crystal-covered Rolls Royce which costs a whopping £500 to hire for 15 minutes.

She said: "Ever since I've been small my uncle has been like a father figure to me.

"He wanted to do something special for my prom and give me a great send-off from school.

"My uncle and family wanted to do something different for me so I didn't have the same entrance as anyone else.

"I even had my own red carpet from the car to the hall.

"The prom was just amazing. The car and what has been done for me brought tears to my eyes.

"I would like to give a big thank you to my uncle and auntie and the photographer and everyone who helped me have such a great prom night."

Isha was ­transported from her granddad's house to the Dunkenhalgh for the special occasion which marks five years of studying.
