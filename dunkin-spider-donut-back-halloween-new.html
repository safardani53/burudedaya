Ahead of Halloween, many fast food chains are getting festive.

Dunkin' has announced that its popular spider donut will return for another season, according to a report from Chewboom.

The donut is known for containing orange icing topped with a glazed chocolate munchkin in the center.

Also, Dunkin is giving its classic chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry frosted donuts a makeover this fall.

They will be topped with chocolate and orange sprinkles.

Customers will be able to order the new items starting October 11.

All of them will be available for a limited time only.

In addition, Dunkin is launching a new big twist on its spider donut.

Customers can now order an inflatable six-foot-tall Halloween decoration that mocks the look of the spider donut.

It is currently listed online through Dunkin's Merch channel for $99.99.

MORE THAT'S NEW TO DUNKIN

The move by Dunkin comes after it launched its fall menu.

It includes several returning favorites including the pumpkin spice signature latte and the nutty pumpkin coffee.

Also, Dunkin has launched the brand new pumpkin munchkins.

Also, the company announced a new range of alcoholic hard iced teas and iced coffees, which is known as "Dunkin' Spiked."

The iced coffees contain an ABV of six percent and come in four flavors: original, caramel, mocha, and vanilla.

Meanwhile, there will be four flavors of five percent ABV hard iced tea available.

Sightly sweet, half and half, strawberry dragonfruit, and mango pineapple will be sold together in a variety pack.

Dunkin Spiked drinks can be ordered in grocery and package stores in 12 states, according to Dunkin.
