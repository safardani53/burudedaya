Who is Margaret Josephs:

Margaret Josephs is a famous Entrepreneur. She was born on April 9, 1967 and her birthplace is Elizabeth, NJ. Margaret is also well known as, Fashion entrepreneur who is best recognized for having founded the Macbeth Collection lifestyle brand. In 2008, she was made an Honorary Judge on the panel of Country Living Entrepreneur of the Year.

Margaret is originated from United States. She was cast on The Real Housewives of New Jersey which starred Teresa Giudice.
