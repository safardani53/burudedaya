TWIN Peaks fans have just one more day to take advantage of its summer drink menu and special prices.

While deals vary by location, one franchise is offering drinks starting at just $3.99.

And those drinks include a variety of exotic cocktails the company announced back in May.

The expanded menu includes a new martini category, six new tequilas, and a mezcal option.

TWIN PEAKS EXPANSION

Twin Peaks - famous for its woodsy lodge-like atmosphere and scantily clad waitress staff - has expanded rapidly over the last year.

CEO Joe Hummel confirmed Twin Peaks hit its 100 location goal this past Memorial Day when its Greenwood, Indiana site opened for business.

“We take pride in knowing our customers and strive to keep giving them an experience that they can’t get anywhere else,” Hummel said.

“Hitting the 100-lodge milestone in Q2 was an incredible feat. Our fans embrace our offerings, and our franchise partners and staff consistently deliver – that’s why we can continue to scale the brand so rapidly," he continued.

The sports bar franchise plans to hit 200 locations by 2027.

Chief-Rival Hooters has over 300 locations throughout the nation, by comparison.

Most Twin Peaks are in the south and midwest, with the highest concentrations in Texas and Florida.

The chain has even opened a few locations south of the border, in Mexico.

Twin Peaks still has little presence in the northwest and no locations yet in the northeast.

Another chain has added a whopping ten new items.
