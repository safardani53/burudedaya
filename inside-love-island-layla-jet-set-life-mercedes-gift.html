Inside Love Island Laylas jet-set life that makes the villa look like a dump - with 45,000 Merc

LOVE Island star Layla Al-Momani has been the talk of the villa since kissing Will Young.

Fans were crushed after Will put his relationship with Aussie Jessie Wynter in jeopardy with the snog.

Layla, 28, is one of the girls who waltzed into the villa during the Casa Amor segment of the show.

However, when she’s not sunning herself in South Africa, Layla lives a very glam life indeed.

The star is a brand managing director who lives in posh Kingston, South West London and can often be seen enjoying lavish holidays on social media.

She likes to post lots of sexy selfies alongside shots of her glamourous life.

Layla was gifted a Mercedes Benz SLK for her birthday back in 2015 and posed beside it while thanking her mum.

“21st surprise! Thank you Mama,” Layla wrote at the time.

She loves heading out for fancy meals too, posting a snap from a lavish Mayfair restaurant on Instagram.

She has also enjoyed some time at five-star luxury London hotel The Lanesborough, posing in the pool in a red swimsuit for a sultry photo.

She also jumped aboard a yacht during a recent trip.

She previously had a taste of fame when she dated pop star Conor Maynard.
