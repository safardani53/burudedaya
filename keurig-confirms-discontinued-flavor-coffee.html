A POPULAR coffee maker has just confirmed that its iconic brand has been discontinued.

Keurig responded to a Twitter user inquiring about it Donut House K-Cups as they hadn’t seen the entire collection on store shelves.

The user was specifically looking for the Boston Cream flavor.

The coffee brand let the customer know that not only had the flavor been discontinued, but the entire Donut House brand is no longer available.

“Unfortunately, we do not carry Donut House K-Cups. However, we encourage you to check out all of our Original Donut Shop K-Cups,” Keurig said.

While that particular customer loved the flavors Donut House provided, it was the price that another fan was missing.

The coffee fan claimed that the Donut House Light Roast was the cheapest pack that the company made.

“At $0.58 per K-cup, this is the cheapest Donut House pack. That's $55.67 for 96 total K-cups,” the Twitter user wrote.

COFFEE COMPROMISE

While the Donut House brand may be long gone, Keurig offers other brands available on its website.

Customers can grab its Donut Shop brand that features flavors like Chocolate Glazed Donut, Coconut & Mocha, Vanilla Latte, and others.

Additionally, customers can try the Green Mountain Coffee Roasters brand.

Other brands that Keurig sells are Dunkin', Panera, McDonald's, Starbucks, Peet's, and more.

OTHER COFFEE NEWS

While Keurig may be discontinued products, Starbucks has just added three new menu items.

The giant coffee chain revealed that the brand-new Frozen Lemonade Refreshers will be joining the summer menu.

Each one combines classic flavors with chunks of real fruit pieces and a swirl of strawberry puree.

The drinks are available in classic refresher flavors: Pineapple Passionfruit, Strawberry Acai, and Mango Dragonfruit.

Each drink costs $5.45 for a medium and are already available, however, prices may vary by location.

Another coffee giant, Dunkin', has added four rewards for the summer.

Each reward is exclusively found on its membership program and are all available in the mobile app.

First up is the free Medium Cold Brew with any purchase, the $3 Dunkin’ Wrap with any drink purchase.

Or, customers can also score a $2 Medium Iced Coffee as a Dunkin Rewards member this summer.

Iced Coffees can also come in member exclusive flavors, like Toffee Nut or Coconut Macaroon.

Lastly, Dunkin’ is also offering a $3 Medium Signature Latte.

All Dunkin’ guests must do is activate the deals in the app before placing their order.

The deals are all available until July 31.
