Who is Jon Hamm's wife Anna Osceola?

JON Hamm has officially tied the knot with actress Anna Osceola.

The smitten duo reportedly started dating three years ago but have now joined the exclusive list of Hollywood-wedded couples.

Who is Jon Hamm's wife Anna Osceola?

Anna Osceola is an American actress who has starred in range of films and TV shows.

She was born in Cambridge, Massachusetts, on April 8, 1988.

According to IMDb, the 34-year-old woman began her professional acting career in 2007 and has since featured in several flicks and mini-series.

Her most notable roles were in Confess, Fletch (2022), Mad Men (2007) and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

When did Jon Hamm and Anna Osceola get married?

Jon Hamm and Anna Osceola got married on Saturday, June 24, 2023.

According to Hello! magazine, the wedding took place at Anderson Canyon in Big Sur, California.

When did they get engaged?

Jon Hamm and Anna Osceola officially got engaged in 2023.

According to PEOPLE, Hamm proposed to the actress on February 27.

The smitten couple have reportedly been dating since 2020.

Hamm, 52, and Anna first made their relationship red carpet official at the 2022 Vanity Fair Oscar Party.

The couple met on the set of Mad Men back in 2015, it was reported.

Anna played the receptionist at the meditation retreat that Hamm's character, Don Draper, attended in the series finale.

Their relationship was kept mostly private until their red carpet-debut.

The Hollywood couple has been spotted on multiple outings such as walking their dog and hitting up California beaches.

According to the Daily Mail, the pair formally tied the knot on Saturday, June 24, 2023.

Who has Jon Hamm dated?
