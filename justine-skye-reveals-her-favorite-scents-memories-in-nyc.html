Justine Skye Reveals Her Favorite Scents, Memories in NYC

Justine Skye, known for her epic vocals, purple locks and flawless makeup and style, sat down with Stylish at the launch of DKNY Stories, DKNY’s new perfume, at The Gallery at The Rainbow Room in NYC.

The 23-year-old songstress and Brooklyn native is the face of the campaign and dished on her favorite scents, reminisced about unforgettable moments she’s had and revealed her beauty and fashion secrets.

The perfume, inspired by New York City and its infinite possibilities and style, is made up of woody and fruity tones and begging for new memories to be created after spritzing it on.
