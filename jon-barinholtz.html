Early Life of Jon Barinholtz

Born in the United States of America, Jon's early life and exact birthdate remain shrouded in mystery, a testament to his ability to keep his personal life private in an age of constant scrutiny. Growing up, he was exposed to the world of acting through the influence of his brother, Ike Barinholtz.

Jon Barinholtz's parents, Peggy and Alan Barinholtz, are both Jewish. Alan is an attorney and actor, while Peggy is a homemaker. The couple raised their two sons in a liberal and humorous home in the Lakeview neighbourhood of Chicago.

Jon Barinholtz attended Bernard Zell Anshe Emet Day School and then The Latin School of Chicago for high school. He then attended Boston University but dropped out before completing his degree. After leaving college, he worked for the Chicago Transit Authority before starting his comedy career.

The Barinholtz Brothers Connecting Through Chicago Party Aunt

The Barinholtz brothers, Jon and Ike, have a special connection through their involvement with Netflix's new adult animated series, Chicago Party Aunt. The show follows the life of Diane Dunbrowski, a party-loving Chicagoan, and is co-created by Jon Barinholtz and executive-produced by his brother Ike Barinholtz.
