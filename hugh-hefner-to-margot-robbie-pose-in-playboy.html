Hugh Hefner to Margot Robbie: Pose in Playboy!

The man who convinced Kim Kardashian, Pamela Anderson and many others to shed it all in the pages of his magazine has now set his sights on a new beauty.

Speaking to Us Weekly at Playboy’s 60th Anniversary at his iconic mansion this week, Hugh Hefner said he has seen Th Wolf of Wall Street and it was “very, very good.”

Moreover, Margot Robbie – who plays the wife of Leonardo DiCaprio’s main character in the film – is “very, very pretty,” according to Hef, who sent along a message to the actress.

“She should be in Playboy! Send her a message! We’re ready for her. She would be great.”
