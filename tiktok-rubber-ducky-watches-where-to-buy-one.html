TikTok rubber ducky watches: Where can I buy one?

ANOTHER day, another must-buy item from TikTok, except that this time it's a watch that doesn't even tell you what time it is.

30-year-old Kevin Bertolero invented the rubber ducky watch, which is about the size of an Apple Watch, but includes miniature magnetic ducklings in a tiny pool instead.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

Where can I buy a rubber ducky watch?

The maker, Kevin, sells his creations, which he 3D-prints, in his Etsy shop, WumboVentures, and on his website, aptly named Watches That Don't Tell Time.

You can buy the "Classic Rubber Duck" Watch for $62.62, and with it, you'll receive:

  • 1 blue pool with water patterned fabric
  • 2 yellow rubber duckies
  • 1 splashy bubble
  • 1 yellow silicone band
  • 1 birth certificate for your ducklings

Kevin states in the item description, "The ducks and bubbles are magnetic, making them fun and easy to move around in the pool, or even onto other metal surfaces.

"People that fidget love them!

"It's also a great way to change how you treat time...whenever you go to look at your watch, instead of stressful numbers, you'll see these cute happy little ducks."

What was the inspiration behind the rubber ducky watches?

Kevin invented the watches while working through his own childhood trauma, according to NPR.

Much like fidget spinners, the idea of a watch that had some sort of moving parts to fiddle with appealed to him.

In honor of his inner child, who was comforted by cute little toys, Kevin dreamt up his prototype of a smartwatch-sized pool on a friend's 3D printer and posted his new invention on TikTok.

There, under his username watchesthatdonttelltime, his video went viral, and his followers multiplied, giving input and ideas, some of which Kevin adopted into his final product.

What have customer reviews said about the rubber ducky watches?

Customers are raving about their rubber ducky watches.

Kevin is a "star seller" on Etsy, which means that he consistently gets 5-star reviews and ratings from his customers.
