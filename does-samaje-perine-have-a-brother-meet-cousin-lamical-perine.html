Does Samaje Perine Have A Brother? Meet Cousin LaMical Perine

Samaje Perine, the American football running back, has only one cousin, La’Mical Perine, and no brothers.

Despite rumors circulating on various platforms, no concrete evidence supports the claim that Samaje has a brother.

To date, there is no confirmation from Samaje or any other reliable sources regarding the existence of a sibling. Therefore, it is safe to say that the rumors are baseless and untrue.

Samaje Perine is a professional American football running back. He presently plays for the National Football League (NFL) team, the Denver Broncos.

Perine was born on September 16, 1995, in Jackson, Alabama. He spent his formative years in Pflugerville, Texas, where he attended Hendrickson High School.

During high school, Lamical actively participated in football and track and field, showcasing his athletic prowess on multiple fronts.

After his high school days, Perine went on to play college football at Oklahoma, where he garnered numerous accolades.

He received First-team All-Big 12 honors in 2014 and 2015 and Second-team All-Big 12 honors in 2016.

In the 2017 NFL Draft, Perine was selected by the Washington Redskins in the fourth round, 114th overall.

He played for the Redskins for two seasons before being claimed off waivers by the Cincinnati Bengals on September 1, 2019.

Despite his ups and downs in the NFL, Perine remains a promising player. On March 16, 2023, the Denver Broncos signed him to a two-year contract.

Meet Cousin La’Mical Perine

La’Mical Perine is an American football running back who has made a name for himself in the NFL.

He comes from a long line of NFL players, as he is a cousin of Samaje Perine, a running back, and Myles Jack, a linebacker.

Moreover, he was drafted by the New York Jets in the fourth round of the 2020 NFL Draft.

After being released by the Philadelphia Eagles in September 2022, Perine continued to work hard toward his dream of playing in the NFL.

He was part of the Kansas City Chiefs team that won Super Bowl LVII.

Perine was signed to the practice squad of the Miami Dolphins in October 2022 and re-signed to the practice squad of the Kansas City Chiefs in January 2023.

Despite being waived by the team in August 2023, he continued to work hard and was eventually promoted to the active roster on December 24, 2023.
