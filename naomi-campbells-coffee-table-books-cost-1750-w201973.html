Naomi Campbells Coffee Table Books Cost $1,750

Gisele Bündchen‘s $700 coffee table book is a bargain compared with Naomi Campbell‘s.

The legendary supermodel, 45, celebrated the launch of her new self-titled two-volume coffee table book set in NYC on Thursday, April 7.

The Empire actress’ collection of editorial, campaign and personal photos is accompanied by an “extensive autobiographical text,” according to the German-based publishing house, Taschen, which was also behind Bündchen’s tome. And it comes in a box custom made in the form of her breasts.

Each $1,750 box comes signed by the model and includes nude snapshots. “When I first saw it … I just started crying because … it was like, ‘Wow,'” Campbell told Vanity Fair of the box set.

Campbell’s celebration had a star-studded guest list that featured Uma Thurman, Paris and Nicky Hilton, Bella Hadid, Anna Wintour and more.
