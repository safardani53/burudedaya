Dancing With the Stars is RIGGED!

James Maslow fans are fuming over last night’s Dancing With the Stars results show, claiming the vote tallies that led to the singer’s departure are bogus.

Maslow got the chop despite not finishing with the lowest score from the judges. In fact, he bested Candace Cameron Bure and Mark Ballas by several points.

Some fans are crying foul, citing a supposed glitch in fan voting might have cost James and his partner Peta Murgatroyd votes. See screen grab below:

Facebook voting, in particular, has drawn the ire of fans.

Claiming votes for James and Peta were instead counted for Meryl Davis and Maksim Chmerkovskiy, the Big Time Rush rocker’s supporters are irate.

But a rep for ABC claims that despite this automated Facebook response, the votes were all counted and counted correctly. No conspiracies here.

According to TMZ, the show rep blew off the apparent voting error as a “caching anomaly” that only affected a small amount of users Monday night.
