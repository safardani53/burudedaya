Who is Richie Havens:

Richie Havens is a famous Folk Singer. He was born on January 21, 1941 and his birthplace is Brooklyn, NY. Richie is also well known as, Folk and blues singer-songwriter and guitarist who was the opening performer at the Woodstock Festival. He was signed with Douglas Records.

Richie is originated from United States. Havens released a hit cover of “Here Comes the Sun,” which was written by George Harrison.
