Sister Wives Kody Brown comes out of quarantine with favorite wife Robyn to celebrate anniv

SISTER Wives patriarch Kody Brown has come out of coronavirus quarantine with “favorite” wife Robyn to celebrate his wedding anniversary with third wife Christine.

Christine shared two photos on her Facebook of her and husband Kody.

She captioned the pictures: “Happy Anniversary Kody and me! We’ve been married for 26 years!”

The photos come after fans suspected he has been in quarantine during the COVID-19 pandemic with “favorite” wife Robyn.

Wives Meri, Janelle and Christine have posted photos during the lockdown without their husband, as Kody and Robyn have been tweeting about this week’s episode.

One fan said: “I think we all know the answer to this... They would use the excuse that she has all the youngest kids. And they're the ones that would need 2 parents there for quarantine to help with the school work and things like that.

"But we all know it's just so that he can be with her for two weeks straight.”

A second suspected: “He lives with Robyn, sleeps with Robyn, puts her kids to bed every night. He spends no time at the other homes."

A third wrote: “Like any other time, he's living at Robyn's, no doubt. But social-distancing doesn't mean quarantined.

“Families are still together, he could still go from house to house without risking anyone else.”

Kody and Robyn living together comes amid their marital issues on the current season of Sister Wives.

On recent episodes, Kody threatened to “dissolve” his marriage to Robyn because she wants to rent a new home in Arizona, while he wants to buy.

Robyn has been adamant about renting, as she feared purchasing a home will stall the family from building on property they purchased in June 2018.

All four wives are currently living in separate homes with their children in Arizona.

On this week’s episode, Kody admitted to “bullying” Robyn into buying a home after he threatened divorce a second time.

In the end, Kody and Robyn purchased a home.

The Sun confirmed Kody and Robyn bought a five-bedroom, four-bathroom home for $890,000 in August 2019.

But Robyn isn’t the only wife Kody has had issues with.

On the show, Christine admitted Kody is still mad at her for being against his idea to build one home for the entire family as opposed to four separate houses.

She told her daughter on this week’s episode: “It’s been so hard I can't even tell you how hard it’s been. I want to build. I want to get out there. I’m tired of wasting money every where.

"I just want to build and get out there and don’t want to move again.”

Christine said in her confessional: “I told her it was going to be cheaper and we were going to save money when we sell our houses and that hadn’t happened.

"We’re going to get back together again at Coyote Pass and that hasn’t happened. This conversation sucks.”
