Costco is selling a 'dupe' of a Starbucks favorite for just $2 - and customers say they 'can't wait

COSTCO has revealed its latest Starbucks copycat item that customers are already rushing to get their hands on.

Shoppers say Costco's new Kirkland Signature Breakfast Sandwich, is a dupe for Starbucks' popular double-smoked bacon breakfast sandwiches.

Costco's new signature sandwich includes applewood smoked bacon, egg, cheddar cheese, and a spiral butter croissant, similar to the Starbucks treat.

However, unlike the coffee shop which charges  around $5.45 for its sandwich, Costco customers can get an 8-pack priced at $15.99 (that’s $1.99 per sandwich.)

On Instagram, an account called @costcohotbuys showcased the frozen meal, calling it a "delicious, easy breakfast idea."

Fans were super excited to try it in the comment section,
