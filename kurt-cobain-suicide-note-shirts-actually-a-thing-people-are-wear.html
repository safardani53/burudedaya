Actually a Thing People Are Wearing

For reasons that defy any and all comprehension, people haven’t just turned Kurt Cobain’s suicide note into a T-shirt.

Other people have gone ahead and paid money to wear this T-shirt!

Last April, Cobain’s suicide note went viral, most notably because it blasted estranged wife Courtney Love as "b!itch with zits."

The former Nirvana frontman killed himself via gunshot wound to the head in 1994.

Just over two decades later, Etsy started selling the above-pictured articles of clothing; and, although that service has since taken them down, the shirts can still be purchased on eBay.

With those donning the items posting photos on Twitter and Instagram, critics have come out in understandable force.

"kurt cobain was not diagnosed with a mental illness and leave a suicide note for it to be turned into a fashion trend," writes one Internet user, whole another adds:

"kurt cobain didnt die to become some cute tshirt at urban outfitters or have his suicide note written on mugs."

It could be more distasteful, we suppose. There could be a sweatshirt out there covered in fake blood from the 1970 Kent State shootings.
