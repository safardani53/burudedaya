Kendrick Bourne Bio | Early Life, Education & Parents

Kendrick Bourne was born in Portland, Oregon, to parents Eric Turner and Luica Turner. Now, you guys might be wondering how his parents have Turner’s surname, but Kendrick has Bourne.

Well, there is an interesting story behind that.

Kendrick’s mother, Luisa, used to be Bourne, but after her marriage to Kendrick’s father, Eric, Luisa took her husband’s last name, Turner.

But after Luisa’s father died, no one was there in her family to pass the name to the next generation.

So to honor their late grandfather Kendrick and his other two brothers kept the Bourne surname.

Kendrick grew up alongside three siblings, two older brothers Evans Bourne and Andrew Bourne, and a younger sister, whose name is unknown.

Moreover, Kendrick had a very improvised childhood; he was raised along with his three siblings in a small two-bedroom home in Portland.

As a child, Kendrick used to help his dad with his work. Not to mention, his father used to work as a landscaper. 

Surprisingly, Kendrick wasn’t passionate about football since his childhood. To be precise, he fell in love with football while in high school. As for his high school, Bourne attended Milwaukie Academy of the Arts.

After successfully graduating from high school, Kendrick attended Eastern Washington University and majored in communication studies.

Likewise, he also played college football and appeared in 53 games during his four-year career.
