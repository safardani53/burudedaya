Who is George Clinton:

George Clinton is a famous Funk Singer. He was born on July 22, 1941 and his birthplace is Kannapolis, NC. George is also well known as, Singer, songwriter, producer, and funk icon who was the founder and a member of both Parliament and Funkadelic.

George is originated from United States. He joined Elvis Presley in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1997.
