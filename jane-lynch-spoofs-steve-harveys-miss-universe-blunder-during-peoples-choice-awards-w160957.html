Jane Lynch Spoofs Steve Harveys Miss Universe Blunder During Peoples Choice Awards

https://twitter.com/peopleschoice/status/684923148614021122

Steve Harvey just can’t live it down! Jane Lynch spoofed the Family Feud host’s Miss Universe gaffe during the 2016 People’s Choice Awards on Wednesday, January 6, held at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

The PCAs’ host, 55, awarded Tom Lennon with a faux distinguished award before a Miss Colombia look-alike emerged onstage.

“We’re trying something new this year,” Lynch told the crowd. “This year, People’s Choice Awards has picked one entertainer who, for whatever reason, has never won a People’s Choice Award but who we believe is deserving of one. And this year, the award goes to the actor and comedian extraordinaire, star of The Odd Couple, ladies and gentlemen, Tom Lennon!”

The actor, 45, then appeared on stage, but in the middle of his acceptance speech, Lynch pulled a Harvey.

“I’m sorry. I have to apologize here. First runner-up is Tom Lennon. The winner is Miss Colombia! … Listen, it’s my fault, I didn’t bother to come to dress rehearsal and, man, I just read the card wrong,” she joked, as Lennon stood with an exaggerated stunned look behind her.
